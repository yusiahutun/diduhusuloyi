Pumpkin Spice Lattes are back at Starbucks so you know what that means: it’s fall! OK, it’s not technically autumn yet but it’s close enough to enjoy the best of the season’s goodies. And that include’s Lush’s Halloween assortment. The brand has some of the best holiday offerings around, including bath bombs, bubble bars, shower gel, lip scrubs and so much more. This year, the entire Halloween collection is vegan, self-preserving and cruelty-free so you can shop without guilt.

The collection is limited-edition, too, so you’ll want to grab it before it’s gone for good. For the 2022 Lush Halloween collection, a few favorites are returning, like the Lord of Misrule Shower Gel. It’s always a best-seller. Of course, there are also a ton of new creepy-cool launches to try. Most of the Lush Halloween collection is out now, but the Sparkly Pumpkin Shower Slime, They Only Come Out at Night Gift and Lord of Misrule Candle, drops later on September 21.
