 Posted on June 27, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1191 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Roofing is a significant component of the Kenyan construction industry. Until the 19th century, Kenyans used slate, wood, and clay tiles as roofing materials. Also, the last century saw fibre-cement, asbestos, and asphalt replacing traditional materials. However, as technologies advanced, the number of iron sheets companies in Kenya grew. As a result, it became easier for these companies to produce roofing sheets in various materials, including copper, tin, zinc, and aluminium.

Iron sheet companies in Kenya produce variety of sheets. They have also flooded the market with corrugated sheets of different shapes, sizes, and gauges to suit every customer's needs. Furthermore, the companies use technologies that make their sheets lightweight, fire-resistant, weatherproof, easy to install, eco-friendly, and aesthetically appealing.

What are the best iron sheets companies in Kenya?
