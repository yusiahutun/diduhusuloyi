Gone are the days when you had to soak in the sun for hours to attain that perfect sun-flushed glow. Now, you can slay the Brazilian beauty look in a few minutes with the best tingle tanning lotions. 

Although bronzers, self-tanners, tanning extender lotions, and tingling tanning lotions are siblings of the same family, they differ immensely in functions. Our firsthand usage of all these products has helped us understand that a bronzer helps to accentuate high points in a person’s face apart from providing a copper-brown complexion. Self-tanners leave no tan lines and bestow a uniform brown complexion all over the body, lasting 6-10 days. In contrast, tanning extender lotions, on the other hand, help to retain the existing tan for a considerable time.

Tingling tanning lotions, as the name suggests, tend to hasten melanin synthesis and pass a prickly sensation through the epidural cells of the skin, providing a natural-looking sun-browned complexion. However, these lotions tend to aggravate blood flow to the skin cells and forcefully exert melanin, for which they can often result in skin marring consequences like the appearance of scars, burns, and red patches in people with sensitive skin.

According to Octavia Lewis, a licensed esthetician, using tanning lotions is better than tanning beds because it helps control the intensity of the quantity and reduces the risk of injuries. 

Choosing the best tingling tanning lotions out of all the options can leave you bewildered; hence, our in-house experts have curated a list of the best ones that are easy to apply, non-messy, and safe on the skin.

    How to Choose the Best Tingling Tanning Lotion?

  • Composition of Ingredients: The tingling lotion should contain a robust amount of DHA (Dihydroxyacetone) that helps to accelerate the process of tanning and helps to produce the required level of melanin that is needed to give the skin the radiant sun-browned complexion without making the skin look too dark. Moreover, according to research published in NCBI, DHA also helps to maintain the required level of moisture within the skin cells as it contains an active ingredient called glycerol. It should be devoid of artificially manufactured skin allergens like sulfates, phthalates, mineral oils, and alcohol, as it may make the skin prone to allergies and tamper the skin’s natural barrier, per another research paper published in NCBI. A tingling tanning body lotion should come with melanin synthesizers, color maximizers, and accelerators to provide streak-free coverage. It should also have anti-aging elements like vitamin C to prevent photo-aging. Lastly, it should also have tattoo protection elements like hemp extracts and jojoba oil to prevent fading due to exposure. 
  • The Texture of the Product: The product should be in a lotion or oil-based formulation and should have an easily blendable texture so that it blends with the layers of the skin and accelerates the process of melanin synthesis. 
  • Skin Type And Texture: The texture and composition of the tingling tanning lotion differ according to the skin texture of the individual. In contrast, dry skin will need a cream that contains more emollients and humectants; oily skin will need a formation that has potent anti-bacterial properties as oily skin has a lot of pores and is easily prone to acne, pimples, and clogging. Therefore, having anti-bacterial elements like tea tree oil will help strengthen the oily skin barrier and improve skin health. 

    • While tingling tanning lotions may help you achieve the forest goddess look for a short span, there are many unforeseen advantages of the product, which directly and indirectly contribute to overall skin health; let us understand them in detail.

    What Are the Additional Benefits of Using a Tingling Tanning Lotion?

  • Improves Oxygenation of the Skin Cells: A tingling tanning lotion causes the body temperature to rise and the blood vessels around the skin cells to dilate, thereby increasing the level of oxygen delivered to the epidermal cells. 
  • Hydrates And Improves Skin: Since blood flow is increased to the skin cells, the skin tends to attain a more supple, youthful, and nourished appearance. 
  • Provides a Natural Browning Effect: A proper tingling tanning lotion would offer the same intensity of results as it would provide when an individual is under the sun; hence, a tingling tanning lotion will help to make the skin look natural without gaslighting UV damage.

    FAQ's

    Does a tingle tanning lotion make your tan faster?

    A tingle tanning lotion consists of high-intensity tanners that increase the oxygenation of cells and promote faster melanin synthesis, thereby providing an instant tanning effect.

    How long should I wait to shower after using a tingle lotion?

    Although most of the tanning lotions suggested above are durably pigmented and are skin allergens, it is advisable to wait for 30 minutes before taking a shower to retain maximum results.

    How often should I use tingle tanning lotion?

    A premium quality tingle tanning lotion like the ones mentioned above would help retain results for as long as two weeks; hence, it is advisable to use tingle tanning lotions once a week.

    Can you put tingle tanning lotion on your face?

    It is not advisable to use tingle tanning lotions on your face as it may cause burns and blisters and lead to uneven discoloration.

    Can tingle tanning lotions be used on sensitive or easily irritated skin?

    Tingle tanning lotions have an intense and instantaneous tanning formulation and cause a slight tingling sensation that may lead to blisters and scars; hence, it is not advisable to use them on sensitive skin.
