HINT: Hidden Rewards

Money and rewards are hidden in lots of locations throughout the whole Kim K game.

You can find money, energy and level points by tapping on certain objects.

The objects can be clicked more than once as they refresh every 2 minutes!

Here are some objects to look out for:

  • Bike
  • Bird
  • Cat
  • Newspaper dispenser in Downtown LA
  • Fire hydrant
  • Tree
  • Wine bottle
  • ‘Now Open’ sign in Hollywood
  • Coffee sign in Beverly Hills
  • Hanging flowers
  • Mannequin
  • Flowerpot
  • Suitcase in LAX

CHEAT: Level Up Quickly

There are 32 levels in Kim Kardashian: Hollywood and we know you haven’t got all day to play it.

So here’s how to level up quicker:

  • Whether you’re at a bar, restaurant or club, buy a drink. Then ask the bartender for gossip.
  • Go to partner’s house near LAX or Calabasas and say hello to them. (It helps if they’re a Facebook friend).
  • Complete gigs that take 8 or 24 hours. 24-hour gigs give you a whopping 800 level points! But, 8-hour gigs will boost you faster.

HINT: Get Unlimited Energy

To get unlimited energy, exit the game and go to Settings on your device (ios or android).

Turn off automatic date and time.

Go to your clock and set the time forward by half an hour to an hour.

When you open Kim Kardashian: Hollywood again up, your energy should be refilled!
