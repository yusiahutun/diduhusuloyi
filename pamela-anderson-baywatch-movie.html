Pamela Anderson Baywatch Movie - Pamela Anderson and Dwayne Johnson Baywatch Movie Remake

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  207 words  |  Martina Birk

Brace yourselves, Baywatch fans: Pamela Anderson is back. The beach bombshell has signed on to appear in the movie reboot of the iconic '90s sitcom—although sadly she won't be reprising her role as C.J. Parker. 

The news was announced by Dwayne Johnson, who is producing and starring in the remake. Johnson shared a photo on-set alongside Anderson writing, "A pleasure to welcome (as gorgeous as ever) Pamela Anderson to our #Baywatch cast. We couldn't have made this movie without you. Welcome home. Thrilled you had a blast with us!"

Model Kelly Rohrbach will be portraying Anderson's character C.J. Parker in the movie reboot which will also star Priyanka Chopra, Zac Efron and an appearance from Baywatch veteran David Hasselhoff. It's unclear what role Anderson will play in the film, but hopefully it's something that will allow her to slip back into that icon red bathing suit.
