See Beyonc Debut a Sequined Camo Bodysuit and Draped Diamond Minidress

 Posted on May 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  293 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Beyoncé has brought her extravagant Renaissance World Tour wardrobe to North America.

The superstar kicked off the second leg of her tour last night, performing the first of two concerts at the Rogers Centre in Toronto. Bey debuted two new custom looks during the performance, one of which called back to the iconic camouflage ensembles from Destiny Child's "Soldier" era.

The pop star's head-to-toe patterned look was comprised of a cheeky sequined bodysuit, a custom sequined cropped jacket by Adidas x Ivy Park, and thigh-high heeled boots featuring prominent black zippers at the back. She completed the sleek look with matching knit gloves, oversized black shades, and an inky wide-brimmed hat which she later took off to show her straight honey-blonde hair.

For the second look, the "Halo" singer looked ethereal in a bespoke micro minidress by Tiffany & Co., made of draped, diamond-adorned mesh strands that gave the piece an elevated naked dress effect. She added silver heeled sandals custom-made by Malone Souliers, and accessorized minimally with diamond swirl earrings and a matching anklet, and her soft-glam makeup look included gold eyeshadow and a matte nude lip.

Beyoncé has documented her luxe custom onstage ensembles on Instagram throughout her blockbuster tour. Some standout looks from the previous European leg include a futuristic silver minidress by Iris van Herpen, a metallic moto-style bodysuit by Fendi, a sparkling maroon minidress from Alexander McQueen, an architectural latex dress designed by Andreas Kronthaler for Vivienne Westwood, and a bee-themed dress and hat from Mary Katrantzou.
