Chelsea star Mykhaylo Mudryk loves tattoos - and even has football ink on his chest

 Posted on February 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  435 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Chelsea star Mykhaylo Mudryk is not shy of showing off his tattoos to fans on social media - and has large ink tributes to both the sport he loves and also Jesus Christ.

The 22-year-old, who was unveiled at Stamford Bridge before their Premier League clash against Crystal Palace, has often posed topless to his 812k Instagram followers while showing off a huge chest tattoo of a football among others.

The £88million signing also has the words 'Only Jesus' with an infinity symbol on his neck, the word 'again' six times on his leg as well as a large message on his chest.

With the football merged with a love heart on the left side of his chest, the right size is marked with the words: 'Dear God, If today I lose my hope, please remind me that your plans are better than my dreams...'.

A smaller inking on his finger reads: 'Thank you God...' With a small cross on his left hand with his religious beliefs very important in his pursuit of success.

Speaking of his body art, Mudryk has even picked his favourite design - which will come as no shock given the theme.

He said: "All my tattoos have a meaning, but 'Only Jesus' is the biggest and most important."

Despite making only 44 appearances for Shakhtar Donetsk, across which he scored 12 goals, Mudryk earned Premier League interest thanks to his Champions League showings - particularly from Arsenal.

With Chelsea stepping in to secure an agreement, Mudryk has said: "I'm so happy to sign for Chelsea.

"This is a huge club, in a fantastic league and it is a very attractive project for me at this stage of my career.

"I'm excited to meet my new team-mates and I’m looking forward to working and learning under Graham Potter and his staff."

Following his switch, many Arsenal fans were left fuming at sporting director Edu for not getting a deal done after weeks of negotiation.
