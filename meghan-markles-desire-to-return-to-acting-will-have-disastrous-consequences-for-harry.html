Meghan Markle's desire to return to acting will have 'disastrous consequences for Harry'

 Posted on July 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  519 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Meghan Markle’s potential comeback to acting could have an unexpected negative effect on Prince Harry, an astrologer has warned.

Meghan Markle gave up her acting career when she tied the knot with her Prince in 2018. The duo walked down the aisle and became the Duke and Duchess of Sussex. They now have two children together and live in their Montecito mansion after stepping down as senior royals. However, an astrologer has warned the royal if she returns to Hollywood, it could have disastrous effects for the Duke.

Meghan Markle’s ‘desire’ to return to acting will affect Harry

Susan Miller, founder of Astrology Zone, believes Prince Harry and Meghan will face new hardships next year. She says it’s related to whether Meghan Markle chooses to make an acting comeback.

Miller told the Daily Mail Harry will miss his old life back in the UK. The royals gave it up to start a new life in America in 2020.

She said: “I think of Harry in a bucolic, beautiful setting Santa Barbara is the closest thing to paradise I’ve ever seen, and it doesn’t have the hustle (and) bustle though.”

She added Harry’s life has “changed dramatically”, but he’s loving his life near Tinsel Town.

Harry and Meghan sent a warning from astrologer

But she warns the pair will come to head over their own career paths, especially if Meghan Markle’s direction is back into acting.

Miller added: “I think Harry is going to miss London, and I don’t know how they’re going to figure that out.

“I think it’s great when the kids are little, because of course, they need places to play, and just be kids, and they’re gonna have the privacy there.

“But Meghan wants to go back to acting… I don’t think the business plan of creating movies is the way to go. I think she will go back to work”.

Miller then warned they may need a “constant supply of money” after airing the royal family’s dirty laundry. She claims they “don’t have any more to tell”, calling it an ‘odd situation’.

The astrologer then urged their partnership will “be tested” in 2024, whether intimately in a relationship or through their business plans.

She explained Harry “who is going through the test” in the next 12 months, and will be the one “finding it difficult”.

Experts claim marriage woes

It comes after royal experts claimed Harry and his blushing bride Meghan were snubbed by a former Vanity Fair editor earlier this year. In the summer, Graydon Carter claimed the duo’s marriage would last ‘years and not decades’.

The comment was said to have upset the couple, with an insider revealing it hurt Harry. The 39-year-old was reportedly concerned fans would find truth in the scathing comment, due to Carter’s previous position.

Not only that, but Meghan Markle’s absence at the beginning of the Invictus Games, plus not wearing her engagement ring, sparked rumors of difficulty between the pair. However, a source claimed the ring was just being sent off to be altered and there was no trouble in paradise for the Sussexes.
