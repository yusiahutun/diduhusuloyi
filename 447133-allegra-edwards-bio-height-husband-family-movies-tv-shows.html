Early life

Allegra Rose Edwards was born on September 9, 1987, in Denver, Colorado, USA. Her father's name is Paul Edwards, the CEO of the company, Edwards & Creson LLC, while her mother is Jeannie Edwards, a housewife. Allegra was raised alongside her two siblings, Sky Edwards, and Evangeline Edwards.

Allegra attended a local high school and later enrolled at the American Conservatory Theatre, from where she graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in 2010. She furthered her education at Pepperdine University, where she obtained her Master of Arts Degree in Theatre and Television in 2013.
