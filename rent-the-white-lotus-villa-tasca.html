You can now rent the beautiful villa from The White Lotus

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  2 minutes  |  311 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The second season of The White Lotus had us all gripped when it aired towards the end of 2022 – and if we weren't booking a Lucia-inspired appointment at the hair salon or hotly debating the fashion credentials of Portia's wardrobe, we were dreaming of an exotic Sicilian escape.

Well now, you can be one step closer to achieving that luxurious Italian lifestyle, as one of the picturesque villas used during the filming of the series is available to rent via Airbnb. You might recognise Villa Tasca from the dark comedy, which recently won the award for 'Best Limited Series' at the 2023 Golden Globes.

Courtesy HBO//HBO

In episode three of the second season, characters Harper (Aubrey Plaza) and Daphne (Meghann Fahy) escape to Noto for a girls' trip away, booking out an opulent Italian home for the night.

Courtesy HBO

The stunning villa is actually located in Palermo – about four hours' drive away from Noto – and dates back to the 1500s. It features 20 acres of beautiful surrounding gardens and four decadent bedrooms, each with an ensuite bathroom.

Adding to the glamour, the villa welcomes guests with a grand double staircase to guide them up to the main floor. Throughout the property, the living and dining rooms both feature grandeur high ceilings and tall windows, filling the space with natural light.

Italian art and design add the perfect finishing touches, including landscape paintings with gold frames, luxurious rugs, polished stone floors and marble bathroom tiles making a statement in each suite. Other amenities include a billiards room, music room, bar, and full-service staff. Fans of the series will also remember the inviting swimming pool that sits surrounded by rich green gardens.
