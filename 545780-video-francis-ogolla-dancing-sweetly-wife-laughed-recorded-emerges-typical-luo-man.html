A sweet video showing Francis Ogolla, the Chief of Defence Forces (CDF), who died in a helicopter crash, was a happy and bubbly soul has surfaced.

The video captured the Kenya Defence Forces (KDF) boss showing off his incredible dance moves with a smile.

He was in the presence of his lovely wife, now widow Aileen Kathambi Ogolla, who recorded her husband while laughing.

Francis Ogolla's living room

Ogolla was dancing to a song playing on his massive flat-screen television on top of a television stand.
