Early Life

Justin White who is living into his forties, is known to be originally from St. Louis, Missouri, where he spent his life with his family, including his parents – Neil White and Ann Davida White. The parents celebrate their anniversary around the 12th of June every year.

Justin completed his education at Parkway North High School. He had his Bachelor’s in Music and Music Education from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. During that time, he also performed at the university concert and worked with several music pit productions. He also used to overlook the student attendance and enrollment in the band program.

After that, he had a master’s in music from The University of Kansas. During that time, he worked as a teaching assistant in two separate areas and taught music to undergraduate students from the same field. Justin White Career

He began his professional career in 2007 and worked for only one company called Kansas City Symphony for around 15 years and nine months. Jeopardy Journey

Justin struggled a lot during the initial rounds of the game. But, as soon as all the contestants approached Double Jeopardy, he turned the game around. He got closer to another competitor Tyler, who was in the lead before the round. Until the same round, the scores were $15,000, $9,200, and $7,000. In the final round, Justin surprised everyone and dethroned the title that was almost in the hands of Tyler. He gave the right answer and had the winning price of $14,701.
