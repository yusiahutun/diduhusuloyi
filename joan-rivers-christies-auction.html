Joan Rivers Christie's Auction - Joan Rivers' Personal Items From Penthouse To Be Auctioned

 Posted on February 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  218 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Good news for fans of the late Joan Rivers: you can now own part of the comedic icon's estate. Personal items from Rivers's New York penthouse will be going up for auction at Christie's this June. The Upper East Side penthouse/mini palace where Rivers resided was a lap of luxury that the comedienne once described as, "where Marie Antoinette would have lived if she had the money." Over 200 items from Rivers's gilded penthouse including French furniture, artwork, extravagant costumes made by Bob Mackie, a jewelry collection including Cartier, Van Cleef & Arpels and Harry Winston and more will all be available to bid on. This is your chance to own part of the icon's fabulous legacy and get a guaranteed spot on Fashion Police's best-dressed list.

Related Story

The Private Collection of Joan Rivers auction will run at Christie's from June 16th-23rd with a portion of the proceeds will benefit God's Love We Deliver and Guide Dogs for the Blind.
