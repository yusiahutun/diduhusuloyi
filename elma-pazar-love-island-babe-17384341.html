Axed Love Island babe Elma moves on with another Islander just days after exit

 Posted on October 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  318 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The Love Island babe was booted out just days after entering the villa and coupling up with Anton Danyluk.

Although her journey was ended early, Elma has caught the eye of a former Islander already.

Whether it was her Essex charm or bubbly personality that won him over, former TOWIE star Jon Clark has his eye on the beauty.

As soon as she left the villa he set his sights on taking her out on a date, and it’s looking like it might go well.

A source reportedly told OK! Online: "Jon was taken by Elma when he saw her on the show.

"The two of them have some mutual friends and as soon as she left the villa one of them told her about Jon’s interest."

The beauty was reportedly flattered and Jon is currently sorting his diary so he can take her out.

Jon Clark famously joined Love Island for series one where he met Hannah Elizabeth.

The pair fell head over heels for one another, resulting in Jon proposing on the show.

Jon and Hannah got engaged during the series finale, but they soon called off their engagement.

Hannah told Reveal at the time: "We just had a stupid, drunken argument one evening because I wanted to stay out and he wanted to go home."

She added: "What’s breaking my heart is that he’s not bothered, the Jon I met would’ve been crying to me."

However, the pair soon moved on and found themselves in new relationships – and it’s looking like Jon could be getting cosy with the newbie.

Elma hasn’t spilled on the reported romance, but it’s likely the duo could keep it under wraps.

It comes shortly after the bombshell revealed if she was in the villa from day one she’d have stepped forward for Joe Garratt.

Elma and Joe were dumped from the show together, despite being in loved-up couples.
