What Is the W-8BEN Form? A W-8BEN form is a tax document provided to individuals by the Internal Revenue Service for them to verify that their nationality and country of residence is not the United States. The form is issued for tax purposes, and it certifies that the individual filing it qualifies for a lower rate of tax withholding or an exemption because they permanently reside in a foreign country that the United States has an income tax agreement with.
