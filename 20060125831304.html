Angelina displays her baby bump tattoo

 Posted on September 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  221 words  |  Aldo Pusey

    With the whole world in on her secret Angelina Jolie is more than happy to give bystanders a glimpse of her expanding waistline - and what's written on it. Jetting in to Heathrow last night with partner Brad Pitt, the globetrotting beauty gave a public airing of one of her seven tattoos.

    The Latin motto etched on her stomach, Quod me nutrit me destruit - or "what nourishes me destroys me" to non-classicists - is usually covered by make-up during filming.

    Famous for her humanitarian and campaigning work, the Oscar-winning actress also has "know your rights" tattooed just under her neck and "strength of will" written in Arabic on her right forearm.

    With Hollywood's hottest couple were Angelina's adopted children, Maddox and Zahara. The Jolie-Pitt family were on their way to Davos, Switzerland, for the World Economic Forum's annual meeting, which Angelina is attending in her capacity as a Goodwill Ambassador for the UN.

    At the conference the Lara Croft actress is to join a star-studded lineup of celebs such as Bono, Lionel Richie, Richard Gere and Sharon Stone, as well as world leaders.
