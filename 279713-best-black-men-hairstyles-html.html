Best black men hairstyles

1. High top fade

The high top fade is among the most famous men hairstyles for black men. The 1990’s kids fondly referred this style to as the box thanks to its fade. Preferably, this style is suitable for those people who love keeping voluminous hair. To achieve the Box hairstyle the top sections of your hair is cut into a rectangular box shape with short sides.

You can style up this hair design even further by flattening the top or doing different variations depending on your personal preferences. For most guys, the high top fade is the ultimate black man hairstyle as it has no age restriction. With a perfect barber, you can rock this haircut for official purposes or even casual events. This style is quite manageable so why don’t you try it?

2. Afro Fade

Afro fade is among the common black male youth hairstyles, and it is widely associated with celebrities in Kenya and the world at large. To most people, the Afro fade is a slight modification of the Box hairstyle. The hairstyle requires little maintenance, and unlike the box, the top section of your hair is left to grow. You can also play with this style by adding dye to achieve that conspicuous modern urban look.

Its unique trait is that the hairstyle allows you to flaunt your rich afro. However, this does not imply that the hair should be unkempt. You can shape the faded sides to achieve the perfect fade. The other styling options include the skin bald, low or high fade, all of which make an Afro fade an ideal hairstyle for black men.

3. Wave

As its name suggests, the wave is a type of short hairstyles for black men that involves the barber giving you uniform wave cuts. This black man hairstyle is still trendy mostly among the youth and individuals in the informal sector. While the wave is an interesting and exciting hairstyle, it is only suitable with certain hair types.

4. Buzz cut

Idealistically the buzz haircut is the most preferred black men hairstyles for short hair. Not only is this style manageable with little maintenance cost but also convenient for the formal events and even sporting activities. As simple as it sounds, the buzz cut hairstyle has a way of making one look sophisticated and sharp if not intelligent. Most presidents have had buzz cut as their hair cut style of choice, right from our very own president, Uhuru Kenyatta to Barrack Obama.

Ordinary Kenyans also rock this hairstyle, because of its simplicity, ease of maintenance, and its classy look. Like most haircuts, getting the best buzz cut depends on your choice of a barber; should be one with skills, right shaving equipment and of course knowledge on different shades of a buzz cut. By doing this, you will guarantee you get a clean, short and smart haircut that will make you stand out in any crowd.

From a distant, this style would be mistaken for a Mohawk thanks to its close resemblance. Interestingly, the Frohawk haircut can accommodate curly hairstyles for black men with curly hair texture. Find different shades of Frohawk for your head and face shape. Also, finding a good barber will increase your chances of getting the perfect frohawk haircut without fearing for your image.

10. Modern buzz cut fade

Finding the best black men’s hairstyles for gray hair can be a bit challenging. The buzz cut fade is one of the gray hair safe havens. Characterized by a slightly longer hair top, and a tapering fade on the sides and the back of your head, the buzz cut fade is well suited for anyone with gray hair.

This style is achieved by buzzing the shaver on the top of the head in a repetitive manner, to get a perfect and uniform cut. On the sides, the fade can be achieved by changing the gauge of the electric clipper, from the lowest to the highest to get a perfect taper. The appeal of the style shadows the grey hair, by making the hairstyle more conspicuous than the gray hair.
