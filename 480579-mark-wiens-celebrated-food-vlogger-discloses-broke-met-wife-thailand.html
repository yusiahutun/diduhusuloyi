Celebrated American food vlogger Mark Wiens has, for the first time, opened up about his life, career, and family.

Speaking to Adnan Huseyn and Chingiz Mursalov on Algorithmpiodcast in Azerbaijan, Mark revealed he grew up in the East African countries Congo and Kenya and attended the best international schools.

The food vlogger then returned to the USA for his university studies, where he spent four years before relocating to Asia.

Mark Wiens talk about meeting wife

One day while in the Philippines, the celebrity YouTuber revealed a friend from the USA called him asking to meet up, and he travelled to Bangkok, Thailand, where they started teaching English.

"We were both broke at that time. We tutored for a year, and during that time is when I ended up meeting my wife and eventually got married and stayed ever since," he said.

Mark Wiens's advice to content creators

Advising upcoming food vloggers, Mark, who boasts of 9.36 million subscribers on YouTube, said:

"Start small where you are and meet like-minded people you can travel with to eat different foods and explore places, and from there, you can choose what interests you and set your goals."

The father of one said when the COVID-19 pandemic ravaged the globe in 2020, he was optimistic everything would go to normal and he would start travelling again.
