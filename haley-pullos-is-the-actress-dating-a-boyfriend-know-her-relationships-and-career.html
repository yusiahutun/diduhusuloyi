General Hospital Star Haley Pullos Faces DUI Charges After Freeway Accident

Haley Pullos, renowned for her impressive acting career, faced a personal setback in June 2023 when she was involved in a car accident on the Pasadena freeway. Emergency responders namely firefighters had to rescue her from the vehicle.

During the rescue, an unfortunate incident occurred when Pullos, still inside the car, had a confrontation with a firefighter. She reportedly struck the firefighter and expressed concern about her "expensive shirt."

Subsequent investigations uncovered that Pullos was under the influence of marijuana during the incident. As a result, a Los Angeles Judge charged her with two felony DUI counts. This incident marks a challenging chapter in Pullos's life, contrasting with her successful career in the spotlight.
