Early life

The comedian was born on August 14th, 1983 (age 40 as of 2023) in Glen Ellyn, Illinois, United States. He grew up in the South Side of Chicago but later moved to the western suburbs of Glen Ellyn, where his mother worked for the US Postal Service. He also has a brother named Devon.

Lamorne Morris’ parents took him to Glenbard South High School, a four-year public high school in Glen Ellyn, Illinois, United States. He was a class clown in high school and frequently went to detention. However, he discovered his passion for acting while in high school.

In 2003, Lamorne Morris graduated from the College of DuPage, a public community college in Glen Ellyn, Illinois. He studied theatre and received the Chris Farley Memorial Acting Scholarship. He continued his studies and attended the Second City Training Center, where he was a member of the institution’s Outreach and Diversity Ensemble.

Professional career

Lamorne started his career as a server at Ed Debevic’s retro-themed diner in Chicago. He later appeared in commercials for several top brands, including State Farm Insurance, Sears, McDonald’s, BMO Harris Bank, Microsoft Windows, Taco Bell, and 7 Up.

After many commercial appearances, he became an on-air personality for BET and hosted HotWyred and BET Now. He also went to Cartoon Network and hosted BrainRush. However, his big break as an actor came in 2011 when he starred as Winston Bishop in the comedy series New Girl.
