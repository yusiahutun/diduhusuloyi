Man Mourns Esther Bobo Who Was Killed Alongside Other Suspects Following Eastleigh Robbery

 Posted on April 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  409 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Esther Bobo was allegedly killed alongside Fredrick Kopo and Jayline Kibe while in Mombasa, where they had gone to buy a car
  • Bobo and the duo were associated with the theft of KSh 3.9 million from a businessman in Eastleigh
  • The young woman was a mum of one, and a man claiming to be her lover, @daqchild0, mourned her in a touching post

On April 10, Kenyans learned that three suspects behind the theft of KSh 3.9 million belonging to an Eastleigh businessman were found dead, and among them was Esther Bobo.

Who attacked Eastleigh businessman?

Bobo, Fredrick Kopo and Jayline Kibe reportedly set on Adan Mohammed on March 30, 2024, as he boarded a motorcycle with the money stashed in a carrier bag.

They shot the businessman before making away with the cash; they were later ambushed in Mombasa.

Their bodies were later recovered in Nairobi and Kiambu counties and taken to City Mortuary.

A man claiming to be Bobo's lover, @daqchild0, took to TikTok to share a video of his snaps with the young woman.

He noted that he loved the late, and she was his woman, and he would forever remember her

Bobo was a mother of one and loved her daughter dearly. She also shared videos of her daughter on her social media page. The young man wrote:

"Forever in our hearts, my right lady. Bobo you have not done well to leave us like that. You will live in my heart forever."

Family of Eastleigh man narrates his last movements

A close relative of Eastleigh businessman Adan Ali Mohamed explained the circumstances under which unknown assailants shot him.

According to the family, Adan was going to deposit KSh 3.9 million, proceeds of oil sale, at a local bank.

When the 26-year-old businessman reached the bank, he found it closed, and he opted to inform his colleague about the development.
