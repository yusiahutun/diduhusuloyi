Katie Holmes Paired Over-the-Knee Go-Go Boots With a Trench Coat and Underwear

 Posted on March 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  224 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Katie Holmes just invented the perfect rainy summer day uniform that also featured the 2022 version of go-go boots.

On Tuesday, the actress shared a snap to her Instagram story in an unexpected outfit combo that included a black silk trench coat, matching underwear, a gray tee that she tucked into the undergarment, and '60s-esque white over-the-knee go-go boots. Katie's brunette hair was gently tousled and parted down the middle, and she embraced a natural glam look.

She posed for a different camera not in view, slightly leaning into one hip. A grand staircase with a black wrought iron railing, a regal column, and marble detailing served as the backdrop for the mini photo shoot.

The actress-turned-screen-writer-turned-director is getting ready to premiere her latest work at the 2022 Tribeca Film Festival. Next week, she'll premiere her new pandemic-themed movie Alone Together — written and directed by and starring Holmes — that follows a New Yorker who travels upstate at the beginning of the COVID-19 outbreak, where she meets and grows close to a fellow quarantiner.

"Katie has created a poignant, relatable, and humorous Lockdown love story touching on common experiences we all dealt with during the pandemic," Vertical Entertainment Partner Peter Jarowey said in a statement obtained by Deadline. "It is pure entertainment that we feel moviegoers will connect with on many levels."
