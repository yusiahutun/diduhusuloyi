For instance, Jeff Bezos, who is the richest person in the world right now, is worth a massive fortune, which his children are slated to inherit, making them rich.

Every continent is endowed differently, and the richest kid in Africa doesn't share the same story as one in Europe. However, the bottom line remains; they are more affluent than their adult counterparts. So, who are these wealthy kids?

1. Princess Charlotte – Net worth $5 billion

Princess Charlotte of Cambridge has beat her young brother to become the youngest richest kid in the world with an estimated net worth of $5 billion. She was born on May 2, 2015, and is the second and only daughter of Prince William, Duke of Cambridge, and Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge. Prince Charlotte is fourth in line of succession to the British throne.
