Hailey Biebers Fashion Photo Dump Is About Oversize Everything

 Posted on April 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  324 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Hailey Bieber, master of It girl street style, is back with another fashion-filled photo dump—and this time, it’s all about oversize staples.

In her latest Instagram post, the Rhode founder shows off four chic outfits she stepped out in recently—each of which she accented with her signature gold jewelry and skinny black sunglasses.

The first look is composed of a black oversize leather jacket, which she layered over a black cropped top and matching micro leather hot shorts by Saint Laurent. (Contrasting lengths is a big It girl move.) She finished the monochrome look with glossy black slingback Saint Laurent flats with a golden chain link embellishment, and a black leather handbag.

Next was an all-white look made up of a crop top, baggy Bermuda shorts, and long socks worn with black sandals. She accessorized with a thin black belt, a black woven tote, her go-to black sunglasses, and gold hoop earrings.

The third look was very ’90s. Bieber pulled out a black tube top and paired it with extra-baggy sand-colored wide-leg pants and chunky black shoes. She then added some of her favorite staples: a black mini bag, skinny black sunglasses, a Nike baseball hat, abstract gold earrings, and a thin gold necklace.

Bieber’s final look was the epitome of her relaxed yet put-together style. The model wore an oversize black leather jacket with a cropped white tee, baggy blue jeans, her Saint Laurent slingbacks, and black accessories: a belt, a mini bag, and sunglasses. She also wore gold jewelry and, in true form, glowy natural makeup and a sleek bun.
