Emily Ratajkowski's Wears a Plunging Black Swimsuit in Miami

 Posted on July 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  330 words  |  Aldo Pusey
  • Model Emily Ratajkowski was spotted wearing a barely-there monokini by her clothing line, Inamorata, while out in Miami Beach, Florida, on Monday.
  • The swimsuit, which is backless and features a deep V-cut, comes in multiple colors and patterns. You can shop her not-so-autumnal look below.

October may be in full swing, but, according to supermodel Emily Ratajkowski, that shouldn't stop you from wearing summer's hottest swimsuit. On Monday, Ratajkowski was spotted playing in the sun in Miami Beach, Florida, wearing the black Encinitas monokini by her clothing line, Inamorata.

The revealing, backless swimsuit features a deep V-cut in the front. While EmRata wore the black version, the $160 monokini is also available in yellow with white polka dots, a neon green-and-black crocodile pattern, and classic cheetah print.

She completed her not-so-autumnal beach look with accessories that included a leopard-print bucket hat by Lack of Color, dark shades, and minimal gold jewelry, including the Square Snake Chain by Missoma. You can shop her look here.

The supermodel also took to Instagram to share some selfies of her day in the sun. In repose on the sand, with her topper partially covering her face, she made all 24 million of her followers jealous of her apparently never-ending summer. "Loves a hat," she wrote in her caption.

EmRata founded Inamorata in 2017, basing the line's products on fashion from the Southern California beach town that she grew up in, according to the brand's website. In addition to swimwear, Inamorata sells underwear, tops, skirts, and bodysuits.
