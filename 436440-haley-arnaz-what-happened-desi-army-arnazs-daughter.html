Haley Arnaz's biography

Haley was born on December 17, 1976. She is 44 years old as of 2021. At birth, her parents gave her the name Haley Amber Charf. Her biological father's name is Gary Charf, while that of her mother is Army Arnaz. Her parents got married in September 1975, but they divorced after living together for four years.

She started living with her mother as she had been granted full custody. In October 1987, her mother tied the knot with Desi Arnaz Jr., a famous actor, and became his legally adopted daughter. She changed her name to Haley Amber Arnaz following the adoption. Unfortunately, her mother, Army Arnaz, is no more as she succumbed to a brain tumour in 2015.

Does Haley Arnaz have any siblings? She is the only child of her biological parents. However, she has a half-sister named Julia Arnaz. Julia is the daughter of Desi Arnaz Jr. and Susan Callahan-Howe.
