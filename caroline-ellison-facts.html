Early Life and Education

Born in 1994, Ellison’s journey into the world of finance and technology started early in her life. Growing up in Boston, she was raised by MIT professor parents who fostered her interest in mathematics and economics from a young age. Graduating from Stanford University in 2016, Ellison excelled in Mathematics and consistently ranked among the top 500 students in the Putnam Competitions.

Interest in Math And Economics

Ellison was an active participant in math competitions during high school, showcasing her enthusiasm for mathematics beyond academics. She also conducted an economic study of stuffed animal prices at the age of 8.

Career Beginnings

Ellison’s professional journey began at Jane Street, where she started as an intern and quickly rose to the position of an equities trader. Ellison’s rise in Alameda Research was meteoric. She quickly rose to the position of co-CEO and by August 2022, she was the sole CEO of the company. The FTX Scandal

Ellison’s career took a turn in November 2022. She confirmed that Alameda’s balance sheet released only included some of Alameda’s assets, leading to a scandal that tarnished her reputation. The allegations of financial misconduct against Ellison had significant repercussions. Her admission of guilt in charges of wire fraud, money laundering, and conspiracy led to her termination from Alameda Research.
