8 Best Cheese Subscription Boxes | The Sun UK

 Posted on February 3, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1199 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

THE best cheese subscription boxes offer a variety from soft and delicious to hard and blue cheese. Want to get into cheese? Here is where it finally happens.

If you can't get enough of eating cheese then a variety of cheeses delivered right to your doorstep every month is a treat that is hard to beat. We have selected the UK's best cheese subscription boxes to satisfy all your cheesy needs.

There are so many different kinds of cheese to taste that deciding which one to try next might be difficult.

If you are ready to expand your cheese horizons but don't know where to start, then these subscription boxes are the perfect fit for you.

1. Pong

  • Pong The Cheese Club Subscription, from £22 a month - buy here

Pong was created with the promise to bring you "some of the very best cheeses" produced by the the best independent and artisan producers in the UK, with a few European classics thrown in for good measure.

For £22 a month, the box contains a variety of hand-picked seasonal flavours. Typically, you'll receive four cheeses of approximately 50g to 200g each.

If that sounds too scary, don't worry, Pong has got you covered. It's your box and it's your choice!

You are able to tick off cheeses you are not ready to experience yet, and to even have vegetarian only cheese in your box.

2. Abel & Cole

  • Abel & Cole Organic Monthly Cheese Club, £15.50 a month - buy here

If making cheese boards is one of your favourite hobbies, the Organic Cheese Club subscription box from Abel & Cole is exactly what you need.

Going from soft and delicious to hard Italian cheese, for £15.50 you're sent a quartet of different ravishing cheeses carefully selected by Abel & Cole's resident dairy aficionado, Ilyas.

Take the first step of your cheese discovery journey and sign up for the weekly, bi-weekly or monthly subscription.

3. The Cheese Geek

  • The Cheese Geek Subscription, £30 a month - buy here

The Cheese Geek is designed for the adventurous cheese lover, unafraid to discover new rich flavours every month, the £30 rolling monthly subscription is described as a "cheese pilgrimage".

The Geek will take you on a journey through the incredible diversity, complexity, and quirkiness that is cheese.

With your subscriptions, you'll get different cheeses every month, and every Cheese box includes cheese info, tasting notes and pairing tips as well as a scorecard to track your favs.

You'll also benefit from their "no repeat guarantee", where you will not get the same cheese twice in a 12 month period. If you’re looking for a unique cheesy experience, then this is the box for you!

 4. The Cheese Society

  • The Cheese Society Monthly Cheese Club, from £38 - buy here

Discover a variety of new flavours and textures, handpicked by the Award winning Cheese Society team and delivered to your door.

For a one-off £38, you'll get five different chunks of seasonal artisan cheese from the UK and the Continent, each piece hand-cut to 200g.

The family run business favours smaller dairies in their subscription boxes, whilst also making sure to feature award-winning cheeses and old favourites that are always a hit.

5. The Cheese Shed

  • The Cheese Shed Culture Subscription, from £24.99 a month - buy here

The Cheese Shed subscription box allows you to support and experience local West Country cheeses from all over the UK.

The Culture box offers the perfect customisable subscription, delivering a variety of cheeses a month to your doorstep, with options going from three cheese for £24.99 to six for £39.99. Tweak your box to satisfy all your dietary needs, with options of vegetarian cheese and pregnancy friendly.

You are also able to choose how frequently you receive your box, as well as the ability to exclude certain items, such as goat and blue cheese.

6. The Courtyard Diary

  • The Courtyard Dairy Monthly Cheese Club, from £29 a month - buy here

Dive in and try new and exciting cheeses, previously-unknown cheeses, cheeses under development, and the very best of what’s in season with The Courtyard Diary Subscription Box.

The Monthly Cheese Club box selection includes three cheeses plus a packet of crackers and a detailed article on the cheese producers, as well as a topical information feature on cheese.

For £29, treat yourself every first Wednesday of the month with the best and most interesting cheeses around. And if three cheeses a month aren't enough to satisfy your cheese addiction, you can go for the five cheeses subscription box at £45.

7. The Cheese Collective

  • The Cheese Collective Subscription Box, from £30 a month - buy here

If you are a fan of multi-award winning British cheese, then this is the subscription for you. Every month, you'll receive five cheeses, ranging from delectable cheddars to mouthwatering blues. You'll get a variety of soft, hard, semi-hard, and blue cheeses.

For £30 a month, "The Usual" is the perfect box for cheese lovers and novices ready to take a culinary journey around the British Isles. The other box available, "The Fiend", isn't for the faint hearted, offering 8-10 cheese each month for £55.

If the deliciousness of the cheese was not enough to convince you, it's worth mentioning that the Cheese Collective donates a meal to a Trussell Trust food bank on your behalf for every box bought.

8. CHEAZE

CHEAZE Box Club Subscription Box, £23 a month - buy here

As an affordable alternative, the Cheaze Box Club has got you covered. For £23 a month, you will receive a selection of cheeses perfect for sharing on a cheese board or cook with.  Alongside the cheese you'll also receive a free gift each month that will compliment the selection.

Prepare to wow your guests with connoisseur-like information about your cheeses, thanks to the information cards you'll get with your order.

The Cheaze Box will give you the perfect amount of cheese to share with your friends or indulge alone.

Can I cancel a cheese subscription?

Before signing up for a subscription, make sure to read the terms and conditions of the specific subscription you're interested in, as each one has its unique cancellation policy.

The majority of subscriptions may be cancelled at any time without incurring any penalties, however you will often need to allow for 28 days notice.

Can I buy a cheese subscription as a gift?

All the cheese subscription boxes are available to be purchased as a gift (for yourself or someone else!), with the added option to hide the price when they are delivered.
