Who is Leighton Meester?

Leighton Marissa Meester, famously known as Leighton Meester, is an American actress and singer. She was born on April 9, 1986, in Fort Worth, Texas, United States. This means she is 38 years old as of 2023. She is the daughter of Constance Meester and Douglas Meester.

Her father is a real estate broker, while her mother is a writer. Meester grew up alongside her two siblings, Lex Meester and Douglas Meester. For her high school education, she enrolled at Hollywood and Beverly Hills High Schools.

Meester made her acting debut in 1999 when she was cast to play the role of Alyssa Turner on an episode of Law & Order. Since then, she has landed several other roles in movies and TV shows. She is renowned for her lead role as Blair Waldorf on Gossip Girl. Her credits include Country Song, Single Parents, Killer Movie, The Weekend Away, Monte Carlo, The Oranges, and The Judge.
