Love Is Blind: Who Are Uche Okoroha Parents?

Uche Ohoroha is the son of Texas residents Lambert and Amaka Okoroha. The reality star is extremely proud of his Nigerian heritage. He might also be vocal about his commitment to improving himself so that he can one day contribute to society and serve as an example for others. In addition to all of this, he once brought up the occasion in 2017 when he was called the N-word.

Viewers of Love is Blind Season 5 were informed in the introduction that Uche is a lawyer and that, as such, he is accustomed to letting an analytical brain take control. However, they stated in the pods that he would be leading from the heart.

In the show’s trailer, Uche acknowledged that having been single for two years, he is prepared to change his dating strategies, particularly with regard to prioritizing physical attractiveness. He claimed that he is seeking a relationship with a driven lady who has a sense of humor and will team up with him to forge a “us against the world” style of life.

Uche has a wealth of professional expertise in the legal sector. Since 2021 and since 2017, he has been the CEO of TaxRobot and Parachor Consulting, respectively. He worked as a project manager at the National Tax Consulting Firm for a few years prior to that. Uche defines himself as an attorney, company entrepreneur, athlete, and someone who enjoys the outdoors and traveling on his social media accounts.

Uche received a Bachelor of Science (BS) from the University of Houston and a Juris Doctor (JD) from The George Washington University Law School. He also obtained qualifications from the Project Management Institute, the State Bar of Texas, the Maryland State Bar Association, and ITIL Certified.

Meet Lambert Okoroha, Uche Okoroha Father

Uche Okoroha’s father is Lambert Okoroha. He was born in July 1952. 71 years old is Lambert’s current age.

Unfortunately, Lambert’s career and job remain a mystery as of this article. He might be associated with A&L Health Care Services LLC.

Meet Amaka Margaret Okoroha, Uche Okoroha Mother

Uche Okoroha’s mother is named Amaka Margaret Okoroha. Born in 1955, Amaka reached age 68 in September 2023.

Amaka attended Abbot Girls Secondary School Ihiala and the University of Houston-Downtown.

Moreover, Amaka is the founder and CEO of A&L Health Care Services, LLC. A&L Health Care Services started in 2014. Additionally, she had also founded JB DAVIDSON INC.

Related FAQs

  • Are Uche Okoroha Parents Still Married?

Yes, Uche Okoroha’s parents are still married.

  • How Many Kids Do Uche Okoroha Parents Have?

Lambert and Amaka Okoroha also share other two children called Kelechi and Steve Okoroha.

Kelechi turned 37 in June 2023. He is an Orthopedic Surgeon and sports medicine specialist at the Mayo Clinic in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

Kelechi was born in Houston and was a competitive basketball player growing up. He earned his undergraduate degree with a major in Molecular and Cellular Biology and a minor in chemistry at Xavier University of Louisiana while serving as a team captain of their competitive basketball team. He subsequently earned his medical degree (MD) at Howard University College of Medicine in Washington D.C. and completed his Orthopedic Surgery Residency at Henry Ford Hospital in Detroit Michigan where he served as the Chief resident.

Kelechi was then selected and completed a specialized sports medicine fellowship at the renowned Rush University Medical Center in Chicago Illinois. While in Chicago, Dr. Okoroha served as a team physician and was involved in the medical and surgical care of the Chicago Bulls, Chicago White Sox, Chicago Fire, Chicago Steel, DePaul University as well as a variety of local colleges and high schools.

Another son is Ejikeme Steven Okoroha who is 38 years old as of 2023. He has been a business owner at Lawyer Up Legal Services since 2019. Steven went to The Catholic University of America, Georgetown University, and South Texas College of Law Houston.

  • Where Do Uche Okoroha Parents Reside?

Uche Okoroha’s parents reside in Missouri City, Texas.
