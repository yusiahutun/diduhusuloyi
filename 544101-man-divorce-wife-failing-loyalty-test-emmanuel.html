A young man, Bright, who works as a TukTuk driver, has been trending after his reactions when his wife failed the popular loyalty test.

The video of the incident shared on TikTok showed the moment the man explained that his decision to have the test done was due to his wife's recent actions, which raised suspicions about her commitment to the marriage.

The moment of truth came when the Ghanaian man's wife, Esther, was phoned and asked to mention her lover's name so he could win a prize from a newly opened radio station.
