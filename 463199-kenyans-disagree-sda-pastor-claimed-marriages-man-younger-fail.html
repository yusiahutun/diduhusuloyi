Kenyans Disagree with SDA Pastor after She Claimed Marriages Where Man Is Younger Will Fail

 Posted on March 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  585 words  |  Aldo Pusey
  • Elizabeth Mokoro is well known for her strongly worded sermons that affect daily life, especially on matters of relationships
  • The clergywoman has crossed paths with netizens after she cautioned women against getting married to younger men
  • She noted such marriages are bound to fail because it goes against God's will and plan, adding that age gap affects the level of respect among partners

A Seventh Day Adventist (SDA) clergy has warned women not to marry men younger than them, insisting that age was not just a number as widely believed.

Pastor Mokoro speaks on marriages failing

Elizabeth Mokoro sternly stated that marriages, where women are older than their marital partners, are bound to fail, and she was quick to give a biblical analogy to support her statements.

According to the preacher, God created a woman out of a man's rib, and this was evidence enough that a man needs to come first in any type of union that is rubberstamped by the Almighty.

Since the Bible recognises a woman as a man's rib, the pastor was emphatic that anyone who tried reversing the original order was taking God for a ride and was destined for a rude shock.

"You came before the man. Your rib is elsewhere. God was not a fool. So if you know you are in this relationship where you are older than the boy quit as soon as yesterday. It won't work," she preached.

See the video below posted on Facebook by Brighton Mcakungu II that is trending online.

Mokoro's strongly worded statements were not received well by netizens, and below are some of their comments.

Mwari Wa Muthoni wrote:

"Please take your primitive views elsewhere, I know of marriages that have lasted where the man was even 10yrs younger na by the way,unakaa kustua watu tu."

Wuodaktari Sum wrote:

"This pastor is increasing depression among single mothers or women who wish to get married to Younger men! But again where were they when their agemates were getting married? Let us support Jadolo."

Vallary Ke wrote:

"I concur, the lady should always be younger than the man for respect to prevail, sina time ya kuharibu na watoto mimi."

Pastor urges younger people to seek advice

In an interview with TUKO.co.ke, International Revival Outreach Ministries pastor Reuben Kanyotu echoed the sentiments of Mokoro.

"It's true what she has said, although some unions have thrived it might not be all rosy as people portray," he said.

Kanyotu added that many young people do not seek counsellors or religious elders before marriage, which has led to many unions where younger men marry older women.

Fundraiser marriages likely to lead to divorce

On Sunday, March 20, TUKO.co.ke reported left faithful scratching their heads after Mokoro claimed weddings funded by harambee have a higher tendency to end up in divorce.
