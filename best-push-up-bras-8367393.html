The 13 Best Push-Up Bras of 2024

 Posted on February 14, 2024  |  15 minutes  |  3041 words  |  Martina Birk

Wearing a push-up bra used to mean suffering from painful underwires and awkward mountains of padding. Thankfully, new styles prioritize comfort and support, while still giving your bust a natural lift. From demi styles that pair perfectly with low-cut necklines to strapless options you can wear underneath your favorite dresses, the right push-up bra can seriously boost both your confidence and your chest. 


We spoke with bra designers and other experts to find the best push-up bras for every bust from top bra brands — Skims, ThirdLove, Aerie, and more. Whether you’re looking for a heavy-duty underwire or a light, wireless style (or something in between), there’s an option on our list that will finally fill that hole in your lingerie drawer.

Best Overall

What to Keep in Mind 

Padding

Push-up bras are designed with additional padding that lifts and enhances your bust. As you shop, consider how much padding your current bras have. Typically, push-up bras with less padding are more comfortable for larger chests, while styles with extra padding can help lift and shape smaller chests. If you’re looking for a more customizable fit, try bras with removable inserts, like the ThirdLove 24/7 Classic Uplift Plunge Bra. 

Shape 

Push-up bras come in several different shapes. Demi cup styles are some of the most common, with shorter cups and minimal coverage that’s perfect for low-cut tops. Balconette push-up bras are similar, with less padding and a supportive underwire for a more natural lift. Flattering plunge bras were made to sit seamlessly underneath deep V-neck T-shirts. And you can’t go wrong with a strapless bra or strapless alternative for formal dresses and special occasions.  

Cup Size Addition 

A push-up bra has two jobs to do, says Newman. First, they use padding to create the illusion of a larger bust. Second, they lift your breasts upwards and toward the center of your chest to make your cleavage appear fuller. Most push-up bras are designed to add one cup to your current bra size. If you’re looking for even more lift, try a super push-up bra, like the Intimissimi Mia Silk Super Push-Up Bra. 

Your Questions, Answered 

Is it harmful to wear push-up bras all the time? 

You can absolutely wear a push-up bra every day if that’s what you are most comfortable with, says Newman. She recommends getting sized by a professional, especially if you plan on wearing your new push-up bra all the time since wearing cups or a band that is too small can cause discomfort. “Plus, a woman’s body is not the same measurements throughout the day or month, so always look for adjustable straps and a hook-and-eye closure for any everyday bra,” she adds.

How much larger can a push-up bra make your breasts look?

Traditional push-up bras will add one cup size, Newman says. If you’re looking for even more lift, super push-up bras are engineered to create the most cleavage and can add two cup sizes, she adds.
