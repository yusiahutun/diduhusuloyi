10 powerful prayers for ladies' fellowship meetings and luncheons

 Posted on March 1, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1253 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Ladies' fellowships are greatly valued not only because they strengthen the bond between friends but also because they contribute to the empowerment of members. A fellowship that focuses on improving the welfare of members is productive, but one that degrades others is destructive. Prayers for ladies' fellowship inspire one to be positive in all they do.

It is common for believers to have opening prayers for meetings. These prayers invoke the holy spirit to be present and guide members through their deliberations. Some ladies tend to be more prayerful than men, hence their fellowships must incorporate opening and closing prayers.

Powerful prayers for ladies' fellowship

The contribution of women towards the progress of society is vital as they are involved in uplifting their families. They, therefore, have to ask God to shine a light on their paths so that they may not stray into satan's darkness. Below are excellent examples of prayers for women’s fellowship:

15 uplifting opening prayers for a women's group meeting

Almighty Father, we thank you for this amazing meeting and for every individual gathered here. Help us be affectionate to one another as you have been to us. Let love be the foundation of our fellowship meeting so that our differences will not separate us but bring us together. Let our deliberations not be of selfish interests but uplifting those in need of our help. Fill our hearts with the spirit to encourage and strengthen one another for your kingdom. Help us to shower all with the love you have always shown us. All these we ask in your holy name, Amen.

Let us be more united and focused on building one another. Just as our fellowship is with the Father and Jesus Christ, let our fellowship with one another follow your example. Amen.

Prayer for Women’s Day

This is a day that brings women together like nothing else. On such a day, it is vital to have a ladies' prayer meeting to ask God to eliminate the challenges women face. This can be achieved through prayers like:

Father in heaven, we come together to give you honour and praise on this women's day. Please help us to live according to your wishes. Whisper your words of wisdom into our hearts so that we may love one another as you desire. Bless all the women in the world to be your hands and feet in spreading your word and doing good deeds. Amen.

Prayers for women’s luncheon

This is no different from the opening prayer for a ladies’ meeting. It is about finding a theme for the meeting and asking God to lead the way. Examples of prayers for women’s luncheon include:

Eternal God, thank you for the gift of life and making our women's luncheon possible. You are the foundation on which we have become stronger and stronger. The gifts you have given us as women have enabled us to bless and serve each other. We pray that you may give us your grace to carry out the mission you have designed for everyone here. Let your word speak into our hearts and fill them with your eternal love. AmenDear father, thank you for the bountiful banquet you have provided for this women's luncheon. We have all gathered here with a purpose to share in the goodness you have blessed us with. We are forever grateful for how kind you have been to us and giving us more than we will ever need. Amen.

Sabbath school opening prayers for worship or church service

Almighty Father, we are gathered in this luncheon as daughters of your kingdom. We praise you, King of Kings, for who you are and what you have done for us. Please shield our hearts from jealousy and the kind of competition that will destroy us. Make us strong where we are weak and instil in us a virtuous character. Frequently asked questions

  • What do you pray for at the ladies' fellowship meeting? Any theme that contributes to women's empowerment can be the subject of a ladies' fellowship meeting prayer.
  • What do you say at the beginning of a Women's Day prayer? First, open the prayer by addressing God since He is the one you pray to, and follow it with a sincere prayer.
  • How do I start a women's prayer group? First, bring together a group of women with similar beliefs and interests.
  • Must ladies fellowship meetings start with prayers? Prayers should be part of ladies fellowship meetings to get God's guidance.
  • How many members should there be in a ladies' prayer meeting? A reasonable number as long as all members can be accommodated in all the group's activities.
  • Must there be a closing prayer in a ladies' meeting? Yes, a closing prayer is meant to thank God for making the meeting successful.
