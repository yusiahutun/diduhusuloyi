Best dressed stars at Coachella 2023: Emma Roberts, Paris Jackson and more

 Posted on June 11, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  830 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Coachella kicked off with some party-perfect outfits courtesy of our favourite stars

    Coachella is back ladies and gents -and you know what that means. Cue the glitter-clad looks, fringe-tastic fashion statements and boho babe aesthetics that represent the apex of North American fashion.

    Each year the festival rolls around, beckoning the best-dressed members of the stardom sphere to show off their styling skills. While it may seem impossible to top Harry Styles' sequin-sprinkled jumpsuits of last year, which you can see in the clip below, a bout of stars such as Emma Roberts and Paris Jackson have already had a mighty good crack at it.

    From Hollywood veterans to supermodels, child stars, actors and more, discover which celebrities hit up Coachella in style this year…

    Emma Roberts

    Emma Roberts decided to shelve the typical crochet concoctions Coachella is so closely associated with in favour of something a touch more timeless. The actress looked ethereal in a long white dress by Prada, which featured the brand's metallic insignia on the chest, delicate lace panels, spaghetti straps and an angelic plume of silk skirt. She accessorised with Monica Vinader's beautiful 'Keshi Pearl Necklace' and served up nineties nostalgia in Melissa Shoe's 'Possession Sandal in Clear.'

    Paris Jackson

    Michael Jackon's daughter Paris claimed her position as edge princess as she attended the festival in a cream satin corset and snakeskin midi skirt. The star slipped into some heavy-duty walking boots for practicality and layered up with a gothic semi-sheer lace blouse.

    Alessandra Ambrosio

    Former Victoria's Secret model Alessandra Ambrosio served up seventies fever in a pair of burnt orange flared trousers by Siedres boasting mirrored detailing and cut-outs. The star teamed the stunning bell bottoms with a cream-coloured lace top featuring long sleeves, an embroidered apple green trim and tie-knot detailing.

    Josephine Skriver

    Fellow Victoria's Secret veteran Josephine Skriver turned out the ultimate surfin' USA aesthetic in a tropical Hawaiian shirt and mid-wash denim shorts. She layered the classic shirt over a white bikini top, crafting an ensemble that exuded retro Beach Boys vibes.

    Jasmine Tookes

    Josephine's BBF Jasmine Tookes, yet another member of Victoria's Secret's OG cult, posed beside her close friend and colleague while sporting a printed monochrome dress. Showcasing a wrap silhouette and a graphic design set against a pearlescent backdrop, the model looked radiant as ever in the contemporary outfit. She topped off her ethereal aesthetic with Haverhill's 'Personalized Adelaide 1 Pavé Birthstone Link Necklace' in white topaz - dreamy.

    Sara Sampaio

    Yet another Victoria's Secret runway star to attend the Californian festival, Sara Sampaio charmed in a beglittered co-ord. The star tapped into the current Barbiecore trend, donning a bubblegum pink bralette and lime green shorts with a high-waisted finish.

    Victoria Justice

    Former star Victoria Justice whole-heartedly embraced a rhinestone cowgirl aesthetic as she emerged from the crowds in flared denim jeans and a black crochet bikini top. The star topped off her attire with a cowboy hat, grey suede Western boots and a hefty dose of glittering rhinestones. 

    Inanna Sarkis 

    Actress Inanna Sarkis was a Y2K dream in low-rise cargo pants, platform heels and a ditzy tie top. The star finished her festival getup with stylish shades and a golden beauty glow. 

    Tasha Ghouri

    Love Island sweetheart Tasha Ghouri was living it up in a beautiful blue ensemble for her Coachella debut. Serving retro Barbie realness, the sustainable style queen and eBay ambassador looked phenomenal in thigh-high cowgirl boots and a butterfly crochet top. 

    Billie Eilish

    Billie Eilish returned to her go-to combo of a tracksuit as she hit the stage at Coachella to perform with Labrinth. The star completed her streetstyle look with a simple black baseball hat.

    Blondie

    Debbie Harry also graced fans on-stage with her glimmering presence in a look crafted from mirrored sequins that dazzled under the stage lights.

    Wet Leg 

    Queens of cool Rhian Teasdale and Hester Chambers were boho colour bombs for their Coachella set. Clasping a gem-studded guitar, Rhian riled up crowds in a radiant wave of cherry red frills.

    Suki Waterhouse

    star Suki Waterhouse brought her A-game to Coachella as she arrived at the 2023 Revolve Festival in seventies-style flared jeans, a crop top and blue shirt. 

    Camila Cabello

    Camila Cabello sent shock waves across the internet as she was spotted kissing ex-boyfriend Shawn Mendes at Coachella over the weekend. Not only did fans celebrate the news they all wanted to hear, but they also coveted Camila's sensational leather look crafted from a chocolate-hued fabric.

    Alexis Ren

    Influencer Alexis Ren was perfect in purple as she curated a fantastical look featuring a halterneck bralette and a mermaid-style maxi skirt. The model and former ballerina was a witchy dream in the two-tone ensemble, crafted from shades of lavender and violet.
