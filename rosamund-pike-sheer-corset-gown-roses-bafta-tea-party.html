Rosamund Pike's Sheer Corset Gown Is Adorned With Fresh Roses

 Posted on August 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  352 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Even with award season in full swing, Rosamund Pike is still taking time to stop and smell the roses—and to help spread the message, she wore some.

Gregg DeGuire

The Gone Girl star looked straight out of A Midsummer Night’s Dream (and like the total antithesis of her Saltburn character) at the BAFTA Tea Party on Saturday night. She graced the red carpet at the Maybourne Beverly Hills looking gorgeously charming in a sheer pale green dress filled with fresh pink roses. The romantic centerpiece, which comes from Simone Rocha’s Spring/Summer 2024 ready-to-wear collection, featured a scoop neckline, corset boning along the bodice, and a wide A-line skirt that flowed enchantingly as she twirled around. Pike layered the midi gown over a nude slipdress that featured lace detailing along the neckline, paired with gold peep-toe sandals and accessorized with only a pair of pearl stud earrings. She complemented the beautiful springtime ensemble with glowy glam, including rosy blush and a glossy berry lip, and pulled her hair back into a sophisticated updo.

While on the carpet, the Oscar nominee took some time to talk about Saltburn, in which she plays beautiful, wealthy matriarch Elspeth Catton. “The script is seductive, [writer and director] Emerald [Fennell] is seductive, her characters are seductive,” Pike told PA Media. “I would love it if this film got love from BAFTA because it is so British.”

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin

In stark contrast to her dreamy floral look, Pike channeled the shameless, entitled Elspeth at the Golden Globes, making a grand entrance in a vintage black Dior gown. The dramatic, funeral chic dress featured a fitted bodice and ample skirt completely made up of layered lace florals, along with mesh long sleeves and a tall neckline that featured the same black lace flowers scattered throughout. To up the grandeur, Pike wore a sheer, heart-shaped net headpiece decorated with black flowers and topped with a pretty little bow.
