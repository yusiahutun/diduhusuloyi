Kabarak University application process

To begin the application process, simply download the form, fill it out, scan the completed document, and send it electronically to admissions@kabarak.ac.ke. Alternatively, you can return the completed forms to any of their designated offices:

How to apply at Kabarak University

Follow the steps below to make your application.

  • Download the application form.
  • Complete all appropriate sections of the application form in capital/block letters and attach the required documents.
  • Make payment of non-refundable application fee.
  • Submit your application.

    • Kabarak University application requirements

    Kabarak University has intakes in May and September. You must meet the following general requirements to pursue a course at the university.

    Requirements for Kenyan applicants

    • Copies of certificates and all transcripts (Masters, Bachelors, Diplomas and Certificates.)
    • KCSE certificate/result slip and school leaving certificate
    • Two recent passport size photographs (write your name on reverse side)
    • Copy of national ID/passport or birth certificate

    Requirements for international applicants

    • An official translation of academic records (where language of study is not English).
    • A current financial guarantee letter.
    • A completed immigration pass form (2 forms enclosed).
    • A copy of travel passport.

    What is Kabarak University nursing fee structure?

    Self-sponsored students are required to pay Ksh. 108,850, government-sponsored students pay Ksh. 343,350, and KUCCPS-placed students are expected to pay Ksh. 1,261,850.

    What is Kabarak University law school fee structure?

    Self-sponsored students are required to pay an annual fee of Ksh. 131,350, KUCCPS-placed students are charged Ksh. 1,011,450, and government-sponsored students pay Ksh. 253,950.

    What are Kabarak University cluster points?

    Kabarak University's cluster points for admission differ based on the program you are interested in. For detailed information, feel free to contact the respective departments directly for any inquiries you may have.
