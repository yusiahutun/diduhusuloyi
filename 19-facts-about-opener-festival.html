FAQs

1. When does Open’er Festival take place?

Open’er Festival is usually held in the first week of July every year.

2. Where is Open’er Festival located?

The festival is located in Gdynia-Kosakowo Airport in Gdynia, Poland.

3. How long does the festival usually last?

The festival spans over four days, offering a jam-packed schedule of music, art, and entertainment.

4. Can I bring my own food and drinks to the festival?

No, outside food and drinks are not allowed inside the festival grounds. However, there are plenty of food and beverage options available for purchase.

5. Is Open’er Festival suitable for families?

Yes, Open’er Festival is a family-friendly event with designated areas for children’s activities and entertainment.

6. Are there camping facilities available?

Yes, Open’er Festival offers camping facilities for festival-goers who wish to stay on-site.

7. Can I bring my own tent?

Yes, you can bring your own tent and set it up in the designated camping area.

8. Are there any age restrictions for attending the festival?

No, there are no age restrictions for attending Open’er Festival. However, children under the age of 15 must be accompanied by an adult.

9. What kind of music genres can I expect at Open’er Festival?

Open’er Festival offers a diverse range of music genres, including rock, pop, indie, electronic, and hip-hop.

10. Can I buy tickets at the gate?

No, it is recommended to purchase tickets in advance as they tend to sell out quickly. Gate sales are subject to availability.
