Australian Open star left red-faced after accidentally hinting at sexy time with husband

 Posted on July 19, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  441 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Australian Open star Ons Jabeur has been left red-faced after accidentally hinting at some sexy time with her husband.

The Tunisian tennis star came through a difficult first round clash with Tamara Zidansek on Tuesday, defeating her opponent 7-6 (10-8), 4-6, 6-1. However, it was the world number two's comments following the match that caused a stir amongst spectators as she revealed her and her husband's plans for the rest of the night.

With the match not ending until after 10pm local time in Melbourne, Jabeur has not been left with much time to rest before the second round.

Asked about her recovery during her on court interview, she explained how her husband will be involved by answering: "Ice bath and I'm gonna make my husband work tonight to have a lot of massages and good recovery."

Her answer immediately drew laughter from the crowd thanks to the tennis star's accidental innuendo. Jabeur is married to former fencer Karim Kamoun, who has also been the 28-year-old's fitness coach since 2017.

She revealed this to the fans in attendance as she added: "Massage guys, massage. For those who don't know, my husband is also my fitness coach and does everything for me so."

Jabeur's protests only brought more laughter from the Melbourne crowd and she was able to see the funny side herself as she let out an embarrassed smile.

The number two seed will now turn her attention to a second round match against either Alison Riske-Amritraj or Marketa Vondrousova after overcoming Zidansek.

Discussing her performance on Tuesday, she admitted: "It was a very tough match. I wasn't playing very well and she was putting a lot of pressure on me.

"She's a player who doesn't give up and brings every ball in so I was pretty frustrated, but I'm glad that I let it out. I just told myself to be No 2 in the world and just win this match. I tried to be more patient and keep it clean.”
