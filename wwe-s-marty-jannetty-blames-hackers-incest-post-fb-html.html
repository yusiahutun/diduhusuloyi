WWE's Marty Jannetty blames hackers for incest post on FB

 Posted on August 1, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  732 words  |  Martina Birk

Former pro-wrestler Marty Jannetty was left red-faced after hackers got into his Facebook account and made a filthy post about his offspring, he announced today.

Jannetty, best known as one half of WWE's The Rockers alongside Shawn Michaels, made the furious remark after an earlier post appeared to show him expressing an unhealthy interest in a woman identified as his non-biological daughter.

The offending post claimed that Jannetty had discovered his once-estranged daughter was not biologically related to him and that now he wanted to sleep with her because 'SHES F***N HOTT,' according to The New York Post. 

Jannetty, understandably, was not happy.

The hacked post, which featured a photograph of Jannetty's daughter, Bianca Marchionni, read: 'If you loves me as much as I loves you, you will give your opinion.

'Just did DNA(2 weeks ago)..she's NOT my daughter..we both held out of sex because you don't do that..but now we ain't????

'From a guys side ..SHES F***N HOTT..but..she's been daugher [sic].. I want to too, but can't get past that.'

In a post made late Thursday afternoon titled 'BREAKING NEWS FOR ALL MEDIA!!! NO SEX WITH DAUGHTERS!!!' Jannetty furiously denied the claims.

'ITS FAKE NEWS!!' he wrote. 'My site was hacked or it came from a fake account, which Im constantly having Facebook remove them. (Had been hacked 7 times b4 since on FB)'

He attributed the dubious post to an unknown thief who had pilfered a phone he had lost some weeks before, citing a photo of himself unique to that phone that had been sent to people in his private message list.

'My biggest disappointment is no one(that saw it) caught on that it was obviously fake and at least ASKED me about it rather than take it as true,' he added, 'EXCEPT, for a few on my fan page.'

Daddy's girl: Jannetty (left) met daughter Bianca Marchionni (left and right) for the first time in 2014, and promised to give up his partying ways to make her proud

Jannetty has posted sporadically about Bianca ever since April 2, 2014 when he announced that he had met the twentysomething woman for the fist time.

'To all the boys who have told me how its life changing when you have a kid...man..had no kids so I didnt get it...now I do...thats my girl,' he wrote.

'And guys, dont you dare look at her twice,,,bad ankles and all I will make sure you never look at my daughter like that again!!!!!!!!!!!..Love ya Bianca!!' 

Two weeks later, he posted in defense of Bianca, who had been the subject of racist posts on wrestling forums because she is biracial.

Since then, Jannetty - famous for his hard-partying attitude - has promised to keep himself on the straight and narrow to make his daughter 'proud'.

While this debacle was the result of hackers, Jannetty has claimed to have been involved in an incestuous relationship before.
