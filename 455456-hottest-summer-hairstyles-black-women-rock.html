10 hottest summer hairstyles for black women to rock in 2024

The versatile natural black hair is blessed with full, thick curls that can form a myriad of beautiful looks. And there is no better time to rock your natural coils, kinks, and curls than the summer. Whether your hair is short, medium, or long, you have a massive collection of summer hairstyles for black women to try in 2024. Which ones are the best?

One great thing about the summer is that the weather is perfect for women who would like to experiment with their natural hair without experiencing significant damage. However, if you want to enjoy the outdoors, you must pick a hairstyle that perfectly matches the weather. The best thing is that there is something for everyone, regardless of hair texture. What are the cute summer hairstyles for black females?

The best summer hairstyles for black women

The summer is an opportunity for you to step out of your comfort zone and try new hairstyles, as there are countless looks that black women can try. But, of course, you would want to have great summer memories, and bad hair days will not help at all.

You must go for something that will bring out feminine energy and look amazing. Check out these summer protective hairstyles that will make you glow.

1. Top knot bun

The top knot bun is one of the best black hairstyles for hot weather. If you have long hair, you can easily hold it up in a big messy bun on top of your head. Then, you can play with it as much as possible to achieve the look you desire.

If you don't have long hair, you don't have to miss out, as you can still create a top-half knot. And the best thing about a top knot is that it doesn't require you to spend hours trying to hack the look.

A top knot bun is a style you can wear for day-to-day errands and even hanging out with friends on the weekend.

2. Twist out

The twist-out has become one of the best black girl summer hairstyles. Why is it a favourite of black women? It is an effortless style that requires you just to part your hair into desired sections and apply a twisting cream to each section.

Then, you separate these sections into two parts that you will twist around each other, coming up with a two-strand twist. The twist-out hairstyle is not only a fantastic protective hairstyle but is funky and fabulous.

Additionally, it is a low-maintenance hairstyle that will lessen the styling responsibilities during summer. The primary intent of a twist-out is to ascertain that your hair possesses even waves or curls all around your head.

Many women have a tighter texture in one section of their head than they do in another, and this style evens out the hair to create a uniform look.

3. Butterfly locs

Butterfly locs are perfect for summer as they protect your hair from sun damage. Therefore, if you are searching for the best beach black hairstyles for summer, this is it, as it is hard to get damaged.

Butterfly locs are a perfect combination of goddess locs and passion twists. It is a low-maintenance style that is quite natural, with curly loops that give it its signature look.

Many women wear the butterfly loc in a shoulder-length bob, but you can have it long, even stretching down your back. It would be best to keep in mind that butterfly locs are a commitment-free hairstyle that traditionally locks your natural hair extensions, giving you a glamorous look.

4. Passion twist

If you are searching for African-American beach hairstyles, you can try the short passion twist. It is a protective hairstyle that any back woman can rock for the summer and look fantastic.

Miami hairstylist Kailyn Rogers (The Boho Babe) created the hairstyle in 2018. The stylist debuted the look on her YouTube channel, and it has now gained traction. Millions of people have likely rocked the different variations of passion twists.

It is one of the most amazing natural hairstyles for black women during summer because you can stay cool with the shoulder-length twist. But, of course, it is not a complicated style, and you can achieve the look in a few minutes.

5. Natural cornrow braids

Cornrow braids are a gift for women with natural hair, and the best thing is that you can be as creative as you desire. You can go for cornrows that create an attractive geometric design or something else. You have endless space for imagination for cornrow braids.

Updo, zig-zag, and curves are some styles you can use to add some flair to your cornrows. You can also blend two different colours to accentuate your look.

6. Pineapple style

The pineapple hairstyle got a new name after a Twitter user published an interesting comment that got people's attention. Different people have retweeted the famous tweet more than 19,000 times.

The tweet went viral, and the style got a new name, finapple. The style's simplicity makes it one of the most comfortable, cute summer hairstyles for black girls.

To rock this style, all one needs to do to pull their natural hair into a loose pony/puff at the top of your head and allow your curls, coils and waves to do their thing. The style's meant to be lax on your edges, so do not use tight hair ties.

7. Jumbo knotless box braids

Jumbo knotless box braids are another excellent look among the best summer hairstyles for black women because of their protective nature. In addition, the style is considered a friendlier version of the traditional box braids since they don't have a tight knot at the base, making them perfect for summer.

One of the most significant advantages of jumbo knotless box braids is that they take less time, meaning you don't have to spend hours at the salon.

8. Bantu knots

The classic Bantu knots can never run out of fashion. In addition, the look is super versatile, making it one of the best summer hairstyles for black hair.

Also, it would be best to remember that Bantu knots are a protective hairstyle as the ends of the hair are tucked away, keeping them protected and unexposed from the outside elements. This protective element is what you need during summer amid scorching heat.

Another fantastic thing about Bantu knots is that you can untwist them for gorgeous bouncy, wand-like curls after a couple of days. You can also add accessories to your Bantu knots to make them appealing. These can be thin pieces of gold or silver.

9. Long braided pony

It is no secret that many women have fallen in love with ponytails because of their versatility. All you need to rock this look is a pack of the most extended braiding hair you can lay your hands on, and you are well on your way.

The style is straightforward, and you can do it at home or get a stylist if you are interested in a detailed look. It is a perfect style for summer since your hair is tucked away and out of your face.

10. Space puffs

Space puffs are your best option if you are searching for football hairstyles for girls that are still perfect for the summer. All you need to do to rock the look is to create two puffballs with hair ties right on each side of the top of your head.

You have numerous summer hairstyles for black women to choose from. Some hairstyles have a chic way of making you look like you put in effort without doing so because they are simple to create.
