Career

Adam’s gift in music was avid from a tender age when he began performing in plays at age nine at the Metropolitan Educational Theatre Network. He was featured in plays such as Peter Pan, Grease, and You’re a Good Man before joining the choir at Mount Carmel High.

Adam would then attend California State University only to drop out after five weeks and relocate to Los Angeles to pursue a music career. He would land his first gig on a cruise ship as a stunt performer aged 19 and then proceed to feature on the European tour of Hair.

The personality later joined the eighth season of American Idol in 2009. He won the hearts of many, including panellist judge Simon Cowell for his Mad World performance. Later that year, he was listed 5th on MTV’s 2009 list of Top 10 Greatest American Idol Contestants.
