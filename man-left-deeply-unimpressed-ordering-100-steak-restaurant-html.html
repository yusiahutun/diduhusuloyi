Man is left DEEPLY unimpressed after ordering $100 steak in restaurant

 Posted on May 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  461 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

A diner has been left deeply unimpressed after ordering a $100 steak in a restaurant only to receive a tiny piece of meat.

Content creator Kidd Fresh, from Tampa, took to TikTok to reveal his disappointment at the portion size in the fancy eatery.

He was stunned at the small sliver of beef he was served and repeatedly asked: 'Where's the rest of it?'

And his followers were also left in shock as one claimed that he was 'robbed.'
