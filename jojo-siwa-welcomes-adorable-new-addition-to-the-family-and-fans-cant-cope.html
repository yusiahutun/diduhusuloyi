JoJo Siwa welcomes 'adorable' new addition to the family and fans 'can't cope'

 Posted on March 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  351 words  |  Martina Birk

JoJo Siwa has welcomed a new addition to the Siwa family, her adorable new dog, Clyde. After being quieter than usual on social media, the star returned to Snapchat to introduce the new arrival.

After starring in the hit series Dance Moms, JoJo Siwa shot to fame at just 9 years old. Since then, she’s become a social media sensation, and fans have been following the influencer’s life ever since.

JoJo Siwa welcomes new dog, Clyde

After promising fans she’ll be back on Snapchat soon, the 20-year-old did not disappoint. Taking to her stories, JoJo introduced fans to the newest addition to the Siwa family, Clyde Juneau Siwa.

The star posted a number of snaps holding her new furry friend, giving fans an introduction, and insight into his day, with some adorable pics.

Fans of the star rushed to Instagram to repost the cute snaps, as one wrote: “aww the name Clyde is so adorable.”

“Omg he’s so cute, can’t cope,” wrote another.

The new addition comes two months after JoJo’s loss

The welcoming of Clyde comes a few months after the tragic loss of her puppy, Tooie (O2) after a fatal accident, just two months after she brought him home.

In a TikTok at the time, she revealed: “My heart hurts so much to say that this morning he was involved in an accident and is now in puppy heaven.”

JoJo’s elder dog BowBow was also a hit with viewers when she first started documenting her life online.

The star has an ‘exciting’ project coming up

Although she may have been quiet on her socials lately, JoJo has many exciting projects on the horizon. Recently, JoJo traveled to New Zealand, where she ‘couldn’t see anyone‘ for a couple of weeks.

The 20-year-old has not yet revealed what she was doing out there, however, other celebrity faces were out there at the same time, leading fans to believe she was filming a TV show.

She’ll also be appearing on Nick Viall’s podcast, The Viall Files soon, so we’re sure she’ll be providing her fans with major tea!
