    FAQ's

    Do hair relaxers damage hair?

    Hair relaxers can only damage the hair strands and scalp if used incorrectly! If you follow the precautions and apply the relaxers keeping in mind the instructions, they won't be harmful to the strands.

    How often can a hair relaxer be used?

    You should relax your hair every two to three months. Also, you should never apply a relaxer to the hair if it has already been relaxed.

    How long do relaxers last?

    Ideally chemically relaxed hair lasts for about 2-3 months after which you have to opt for another session.

    How can relaxed hair be kept healthy?

    Opting for hydrating products after the treatment is the key! Choose a hydrating shampoo, conditioner, serum, or mask, in order to keep the mane moisturized and healthy.

    What should I do if I experience burning or discomfort during the application process?

    If you experience a burning sensation or discomfort during the relaxing process, wash the area immediately.

    Can you use color after using a hair relaxer?

    It is recommended to wait for at least one week and one wash after opting for a relaxer service before you opt for permanent hair color.

    How relaxed hair can be maintained?

    Cleanse your hair and scalp with a mild and sulfate-free shampoo twice a week. Avoid heat styling and incorporate a moisturizing hair mask after washing!

    What is the aftercare after using a relaxer?

    Try not to get the hair wet for at least 48 hours in order to increase the longevity of the effectiveness.

    Is it necessary to go to a professional hairstylist to apply a hair relaxer?

    Not at all! You can opt for the same in the comfort of your home once you know how to apply them carefully!

    Are there any natural alternatives to traditional hair relaxers?

    Yes, there are less harsh alternatives like keratin treatments or texturizers that soften and smooth your curls without damaging the strands.

    What can be done if the hair is breaking or shedding after a relaxer treatment?

    Opt for protein treatments and deep conditioning treatments to prevent the breakage and shedding of your hair after the relaxing treatment.
