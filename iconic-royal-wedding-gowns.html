1

Prince Christian of Hanover & Alessandra de Osma

Getty Images

Lawyer and former model Alessandra de Osma met Prince Christian of Hanover (son of Ernst August, Prince of Hanover) when she was his tour guide in Peru. The two were married in Lima, Peru in late 2017. De Osma wore an all-lace, mock-necked gown by Jorge Vázquez, and the Hanover Floral Tiara, which was previously worn by Princess Caroline of Hanover (formerly Princess Caroline of Monaco) at a gala in 2004, and Ekaterina Malysheva (Prince Ernst August Jr. of Hanover's wife and de Osma's sister-in-law) at her own wedding in 2017, Hello reports.

2

Prince Ernst August Jr. & Ekaterina Malysheva

Getty Images

Prince Ernst August Jr., stepson to Princess Caroline of Monaco and son of Ernst August, Prince of Hanover married fashion designer Ekaterina Malysheva in Hanover, Germany in July 2017. While his father refused to attend the wedding due to a financial dispute, the bride's all-over lace gown embroidered with pearls and beadwork–created by the bride's friend, designer Sandra Mansour–made a breathtaking appearance.

Lady Charlotte Wellesley & Alejandro Santo Domingo

Getty Images

Lady Charlotte Wellesley, daughter of Princess Antonia, Duchess of Wellington, a descendant of Queen Victoria, married financier Alejandro Santo Domingo in Íllora, Spain in May 2016. The bride wore a bespoke Emilia Wickstead bridal gown with an off-the-shoulder neckline and a jaw-dropping train, accessorized with a veil scattered with pearls.

Beatrice Borromeo & Pierre Casiraghi

Getty Images

Pierre Casiraghi, the second son of Caroline, Princess of Hanover, married Beatrice Borromeo, descendant of the Italian aristocratic House of Borromeo in three distinct ceremonies in 2015. Here, the bride wore a bohemian sheath with bell sleeves by designer Alberta Ferretti, capped off with a flower crown.

Princess Maria Carolina Christina of Bourbon-Parma, Marchioness of Sala & Albert Alphons Ludgerus Brenninkmeijer

Prince Guillaume & Princess Stéphanie of Luxembourg

Princess Charlene & Prince Albert II of Monaco

Getty Images

Princess Charlene and Prince Albert II of Monaco were married in 2011. While royal wedding gowns typically incorporate lace, a full skirt and an epic train, this royal went minimal, sleek and chic. The princess opted for a custom wedding dress by Armani Privé with an off-the-shoulder neckline and a slim silhouette.

King Jigme Khesar Namgyel Wangchuck & Queen Jetsun Pema of Bhutan

Getty Images

Of the Queen, the King said (via InStyle), "I have been waiting for quite some time to get married. But it doesn’t matter when you get married as long as it is to the right person. I am certain I am married to the right person."
