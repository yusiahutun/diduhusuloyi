Vincent Irizarry Career

Vincent Irizarry got a chance to perform in daytime television in 1983 as Brandon “Lujack” Luvonaczek and Nick McHenry Spaulding on an American television soap opera Guiding Light. For his role in the series, he got Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Younger Actor in a Drama Series nomination.

Later in 1987, Vincent played in the American television soap opera Santa Barbara as Scott Clark alongside Ethan Asher, Marcello Armonti, Andrea Bedford and many others. Likewise, in 1997 Irizarry played in another American television soap opera All My Children as David Hayward. In the series, he appeared alongside Rebecca Budig, Daniel Cosgrove, Michael E. Knight, and many others.

For his role in All My Children, Vincent won Soap Opera Digest Awards for Outstanding Villain. In addition, his other TV series and films credits are One Life to Live, The Young and the Restless, Days of Our Lives, Circus, Lying Eyes and many others.
