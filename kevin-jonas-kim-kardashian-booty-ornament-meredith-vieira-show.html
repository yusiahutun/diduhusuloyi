It seems Kevin Jonas wants Kim Kardashian under his Christmas tree this year ... or at least hanging on it!

During an appearance on "The Meredith Vieira Show" this week, the singer shared a special hand-made ornament he crafted to resemble Kim's infamous naked Paper magazine cover photo.

"I know you wanted to do something special as a dad for his little girl for her first Christmas," host Meredith Vieira said to the audience. "What did you make her?"

"I handcrafted an ornament, it's very in season this year," he said. "I actually brought one for you. I thought it would be appropriate for you, it's very the to-do this season. I thought that would work for you. Hopefully you like it."

Then the former JoBro presented Vieira with the killer Christmas tree accessory: A printout of Kim's infamous pic with two gold ornaments where her booty should be. "I love it," Vieira gushed, adding, "What message are you trying to send to [newborn daughter] Alena?"

"I'm not too sure yet. All she did was look at it very confused and try to grab one of the balls," he said. "I'm sure that's happened before," Vieira joked.
