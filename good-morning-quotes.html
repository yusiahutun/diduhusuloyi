62 'Good Morning' Quotes | First For Women

 Posted on February 26, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1168 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Rise and shine! As you sip your hot cup of joe this morning, take a moment to browse these uplifting and inspiring “good morning” quotes. Not only will they put a smile on your face and start your day off with a positive outlook, Suzanne Degges-White, Ph.D., a counselor and professor at Northern Illinois University, says that simply reading positive quotes and looking at the colorful images surrounding them reduces stress, boosts joy and balances your mood. The best part? The blissful benefits can last for hours.

Bonus: Try sharing one quote a day with a friend to multiply the feel-good effect. To kick your day off right an to make them easier to like and share with loved ones, we’ll also be posting one each morning on our FIRST For Women Facebook page.
