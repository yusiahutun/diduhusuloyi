About Amanda's Biography:

Amanda Plummer is an American actress who was born on the 23rd of March, 1957 in New York City of United States. She was born in a family of actors, and it is not surprising that she turned out to become one.

She was the only child of actors Christopher Plummer and Tammy Grimes and her family ancestry consists of former prime minister of Canada; John Joseph Caldwell Abbott.

Although Amanda was intended to become a jockey as a child because of her fascination towards horses, she later thought about working out as an actor; inspired by the success and lifestyle of her own parents.

Having appeared in over a hundred different TV shows and movies, she is best known for her work on stage and has appeared in plenty of theatre acts; both Broadway and off-Broadway.

Her Broadway debut came in 1981 in A Taste of Honey, although she had been acting on stage for a long time before that. It was in the early 80s that she established herself as a professional actress, and also started to appear in TV shows as well as movies.

With her long and successful career, some of the highlights would be when she won awards like Tony Awards, Boston Critics Circle Awards, and Emmy Awards as well.
