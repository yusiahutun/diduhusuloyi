Holly Sent Away To New Facility, Will This Offer Nicole New Hope?

 Posted on May 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  392 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers suggest that a major cast change is coming on the horizon. Holly Jonas (Ashley Puzemis) is currently in a coma due to an overdose.

While the staff at Salem Hospital are doing all they can to pull her out of this nightmare, they simply may not have the cutting-edge equipment needed to help Holly recover.

Looks like Ms. Jonas will be heading out of town for a little bit, but how will Nicole Walker-DiMera (Arianne Zucker) feel about this all?

DOOL Spoilers: Holly Jonas Heads To A Facility

Days spoilers for the week of January 11th hint that Holly will have a turning point. It looks like there’s hope for a full recovery; however, she’ll need state-of-the-art treatment for this to happen.

As such, it’ll be recommended that she go to another facility to receive care. Sounds like Nicole will want to go with her, but doctors will reassure momma bear that the best thing for Holly is to go on her own so that the medical team can focus on her recovery without any interruptions.

Days Spoilers: Goodbye For Now …

DOOL spoilers note that Holly will be prepped and ready to leave Salem on Friday, January 19th. Nicole will be joined by Holly’s grandmother Maggie Kiriakis (Suzanne Rogers), stepdad EJ DiMera (Dan Feuerriegel), and adoptive dad, Eric Brady (Greg Vaughan) to say goodbye.

The bad news is that Holly is leaving the canvas; however, the good news is that it won’t be for too long. Added teasers imply that actress Ashley Puzemis has filmed more scenes for the soap, and it looks like the Holly-Tate Black (Jamie Martin Mann) love story will resume at some point.

This exit is for storyline purposes, and likely to keep Tate’s legal woes rolling. After all, if Holly wakes up, she’ll fess up to what really happened on New Year’s Eve, which would exonerate Tate of all charges.

Right now, he’s struggling with the cards that have been dealt to him and has landed in prison. Seems like this nightmare won’t soon end for young Tate.
