Music royalties

Tyga has worked for several labels, including Young Money Entertainment, Republic Records, Cash Money Records, and G.O.O.D. Music. They pay him royalties.

In 2018, Tyga sued Young Money claiming $10 million in damages for unpaid royalties. Cash Money and Young Money agreed to pay all royalties they owed him in 2016. Tyga sued the labels for breach of contract when they did not meet his interests.

Tyga's record label

After the fallout with Young Money, Tyga established an independent music label called the Last Kings Records in 2015. In 2017, Tyga signed a multi-million dollar deal with EMPIRE to distribute the label's music.
