Amouranth 'schools' accountant after he mocks hot tub streamer's petrol station purchase

 Posted on April 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  416 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The viral Twitch streamer Amouranth has hit back at a qualified accountant for being a 'keyboard warrior' after he called her decision to buy a petrol station a 'fantasy'.

Amouranth, real name Kaitlyn Siragusa, announced last week that she has bought a Circle K petrol station for $4 million USD in order to get a tax break on her earnings.

Amouranth, who is known for her 'hot tub' channel and makes millions off of Twitch and OnlyFans, took to Twitter to explain that the petrol station purchase ended up giving her a $1,110,000 tax break due to local income tax laws.

As she only invested $1 million and borrowed the rest, Amouranth claimed she is able to pocket the $110,000 from the taxman and pay off the remaining $3 million loan using the value generated by the petrol station.
