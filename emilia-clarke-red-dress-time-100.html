Emilia Clarke Wears Red Sheer Dolce &amp; Gabbana Dress to the Time 100 Gala

 Posted on September 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  363 words  |  Aldo Pusey
  • Game of Thrones star Emilia Clarke stunned in a bright red Dolce & Gabbana tulle gown at the Time 100 gala in New York on Tuesday night.
  • The actress also reunited with former GoT co-star Richard Madden, better known as Robb Stark in the hit HBO fantasy series.

Emilia Clarke ditched Daenerys Targaryen's fur coat for a fiery red (and slightly sheer) Dolce & Gabbana gown at the Time 100 gala in New York City last night. The Game of Thrones star shone on the red carpet in a tulle, sleeveless number with a bustier top and thick silky waistband.

Dimitrios Kambouris//Getty Images

The look is from Dolce & Gabbana's Fall 2019 collection, which just hit the runways in Milan in February.

Victor VIRGILE//Getty Images

Clarke stuck to the monochrome theme with a crimson lip, drop earrings, and red Christian Louboutin slingback heels. She also kept her brunette bob pristinely tucked behind her ears.

ANGELA WEISS//Getty Images

Inside the venue, Clarke had a Game of Thrones reunion with former co-star Richard Madden, who played Robb Stark (the O.G. King in the North) for the first three seasons. They actors never shared a scene in the show, but they've made a number of press appearances together in the past. The two were seen at the same table as Rami Malek and Time magazine's editorial director Dan Macsai.

Patrick McMullan//Getty Images

Clarke is one of the honorees in Time's list of 100 most influential people this year. Her tribute was written by Emma Thompson, who penned the rom-com Last Christmas, which Clarke will appear in later this year. Thompson describes the actress as "a powerhouse to watch and an ally to die for."
