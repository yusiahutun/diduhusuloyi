 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  449 words  |  Martina Birk

Darcey Silva and Georgi Rusev of 90 Day Fiancé fame have tied the knot.

The news was revealed via Instagram on Thursday in a joint post between Silva, 49, and TLC.

'I’m thrilled to share that Georgi and I got married on our special day of November 11th,' the blonde television personality stated in the caption.

A photo showed the newlywed couple's hands together as they wore their wedding rings.

Rusev's band boasted diamonds and black gemstones and Silva's was square-shaped with a blue stone surrounded by diamonds.

The social media caption continued: 'I’ve been on a long journey to find love, and in the end, love wins.'

'I’m so blessed for you all to be a part of my journey in life, love, and happiness. Thank you for all the love and support! Stay tuned. Love, Darcey xoxo,' the note concluded.

Silva personalized her veil by adding the initials of her and Rusev’s first names embroidered. 

The duo's 90 Day Fiancé co-stars shared congratulatory messages in the comments section of the post.

Thaís Ramone wrote, 'Ahhh happy for you! Congrats ❤️❤️.'

And Armando Niedermeier-Rubio added a warm, 'Congratulations,' while Anny Francisco said the same.

One of Silva's two daughters, Aniko Tai Bollok, supported her mom as she added an encouraging 'Slayyyyyy.' 

In addition to Aniko, Darcey shares Aspen with ex-husband Frank Bollok.

Some fans were left confused by the marriage news. 'What??? I think I missed a few chapters,' one person commented. Someone else echoed the sentiment, writing, 'Whattttt?!! I didn’t think they were back together! Well congrats to you both!'

The couple had called off their engagement for a second time in February 2022. Rusev initially popped the question in June 2020, during the season one finale of Darcey & Stacey, Silva's reality show with her twin sister.

Rusev’s ex-wife Octavia Bellinger sabotaged the couple's relationship in 2021's season two of the show as she revealed she'd found him searching for sugar mamas online while they were together.

Georgi and Darcey split before reconciling, and then he proposed again in season three of the series. February 2022 saw them breakup again. Then, earlier this year, Rusev captioned a social media photo of himself in a tuxedo: 'It’s finally happening.'

Darcey also hinted at their nuptials when she shared on her Instagram Story that she was having a dress designed by Katerina Bocci.
