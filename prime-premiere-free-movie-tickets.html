What is Prime Premiere?

Prime Premiere is Amazon’s new feature where Prime members can watch new Amazon original movies and TV series at their local movie theater for free. Yes: Free.

Each Prime account receives one reservation to one movie theater per Prime Premiere event. Those events include screenings to Amazon originals such as Sitting in the Bars With Cake, A Million Miles Away, Gen V, The Burial, and Candy Cane Lane.

Each reservation comes with two free tickets: one ticket for the Prime account holder and one for their guest. Each ticket also comes with a free small popcorn and a free small soda or water at the theater. Select screenings also include photo opps and special giveaways for Prime members and their guests.

How to use Prime Premiere for free movie tickets

Read on for step-by-step instructions for how to use Prime Premiere to reserve free movie tickets.

  • Visit PrimePremiere.Amazon
  • Click “Reserve Tickets” for the Prime Premiere event of your choice and select a theater near you
  • Click on the 7 p.m. button under the theater of your choice and follow the prompts to log in with your Amazon Prime account
  • Select your number of tickets — 1 or 2 per Prime account
  • Download the ticket PDFs that appear
  • On the day of your Prime Premiere event, check in at your theater starting at 6:15 p.m. local time
  • Enjoy the Prime Premiere screening starting at 7 p.m. local time
  • Note: If you forgot to download your PDF, visit PrimePremiere.Amazon and go to the showtimes ticketing page. There, click “Already booked? Manage tickets here” and log into your Amazon Prime account. A confirmation will then pop up that lists your name, the theater where you reserved your tickets, and another download option.

    How long is Prime Video’s free trial?

    Prime Video’s free trial is four times longer than most other streaming services. Compared to Paramount Plus, which offer just a seven-day free trial, Amazon Prime Video’s free trial is 30 days. That’s three weeks more than competitors. The free trial also comes with the same benefits of Prime, such as free two-day shipping, Prime Music and more. But perhaps the best part about Prime Video’s free trial is that customers can sign up for it multiple times. According to Amazon, users can sign up for Prime Video’s free trial over and over again as long as it isn’t within the same 12 months. This means that if you if you haven’t been an Amazon Prime member for more than 12 months, you’re eligible to receive another 30 days for free.

    How much is Prime Video?

    Prime Video costs $8.99 per month for non-Amazon Prime members. Prime Video comes for free with a Prime subscription, which also offers a 30-day free trial and costs $14.99 per month or $139 per year (a saving of about $36 from the monthly subscription.) Along with a free subscription to Prime Video, Prime also includes Prime Delivery (such as free two-day shipping, free same-day shipping, free release-date delivery and free no-rush shipping), as well as access to Prime Music, Prime Gaming and Prime Reading. Students can also subscribe to Amazon Prime Student for $7.49 per month or $69 per year, which is half the price of the regular subscription and also comes with Prime Video.
