Travis Scott Shares Rare Sweet Photo of Daughter Stormi at Home

 Posted on April 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  324 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Between shows, Travis Scott is spending precious time with his daughter, Stormi Webster, at home.

Yesterday the rapper (born Jacques Bermon Webster II) shared a rare glimpse into his personal life via some photos on his Instagram Story. In one, he shows Stormi looking adorable while baking in his kitchen. She wears a little blue pajama set and smiles at the camera while dipping her hands in some flour.

Courtesy of Travis Scott via Instagram

Scott didn’t provide any context for the sweet image, but it’s a rare move for him, as he rarely shares anything from his personal life, especially photos, on social media.

The “Sicko Mode” rapper shares Stormi and son Aire with ex Kylie Jenner. The two began dating on and off in 2017 and last broke things off in late 2022, with Jenner spending the holidays with her family and the pair’s kids.

Though there have been rumors of a possible reconciliation between the two since the split—especially after Scott left a flirty comment on one of Jenner’s Instagram posts, and after the two attended Stormi’s kindergarten graduation together—a source told TMZ this June that Jenner and Scott are only interested in co-parenting their children. This breakup feels more “permanent,” the insider said, adding that the stars are not working to get back together.

Scott is preparing for his Utopia tour, which will include shows around Europe and across the U.S. “Utopia tour soon U.S. and Euro dates soon to be announce [sic] I miss the road and I miss y’all,” he wrote on Instagram earlier this month.
