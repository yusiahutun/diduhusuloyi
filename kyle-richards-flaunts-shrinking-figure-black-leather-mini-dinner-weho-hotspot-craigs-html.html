Kyle Richards flaunts shrinking figure in black leather mini for dinner at WeHo hotspot Craig's

 Posted on July 9, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  909 words  |  Martina Birk

The last remaining original Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Kyle Richards flaunted her shrinking 5ft2in figure while clad in a black leather pleated mini-skirt to grab an early dinner at West Hollywood hotspot Craig's on Monday.

The 54-year-old mother-of-four's physical transformation began with breast reduction surgery last May and, by July 15, she decided to quit alcohol, sugar, and carbs.

'I started taking it to the next level by working out two hours a day,' Kyle told DailyMail.com back on February 1.

'I really make a point to eat well and take care of my body, and I feel like it shows.'

Richards stressed: 'When people try to blame my appearance on surgery or Ozempic, which would be completely irresponsible to use when diabetics can't get their hands on it, it's really frustrating to me. It bothers me. I'd rather just inspire people with what I'm doing so they can try the same things I’ve been doing and see what happens to them.'

Semaglutide injectables like Ozempic, Wegovy, Rybelsus, and Victoza should only be prescribed to severely overweight patients suffering from type 2 diabetes - but the drug has become so overprescribed there's been shortages.

The Hollywood-born socialite completed her casual attire with a blue-jean jacket over a Queen Elizabeth T-shirt, black leather knee-high boots, and a matching Hermès Birkin bag - created 1984 in honor of Jane Birkin - which can cost between $5K-$500K.

On Sunday, Kyle - who boasts 6.4M social media followers - took to Instastory to detail her work-out consisting of 'one hour alternating between walking on a 10% incline and 3.7mph and running flat at 6mph' as well as 'ab work and arms.'

Richards' fitness trainer Cory Gregory revealed that she's been taking part in his 60-minute 'Cardio Shredz Plan - Cover Model Sunday Cardio Workout.'

On Sunday, the aunt of Paris Hilton slipped into a LBD and snapped a picture with four friends including E! News host Justin Sylvester, 'accountability' coach/stylist Jenn Leipart, and Faye Resnick (best known for the OJ Simpson trial).

Sylvester had just interviewed Kyle's second husband Mauricio Umansky about the upcoming season two of Netflix's Buying Beverly Hills as well as his new book The Dealmaker, which hit shelves last Tuesday.

Richards has four daughters - Alexia Umansky, 26; Sophia Umansky, 23; Portia Umansky, 15; and Farrah Aldjufrie, 34 - from her marriages to the 52-year-old real estate mogul and first husband Guraish Aldjufrie.

The Halloween Ends actress has been hard at work filming the 13th season of her Bravo reality series alongside Sutton Stracke, Garcelle Beauvais, Dorit Kemsley, Erika 'Jayne' Girardi, Crystal Kung Minkoff, and newcomer Annemarie Wiley.
