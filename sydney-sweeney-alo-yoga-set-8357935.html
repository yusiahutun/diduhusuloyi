Sydney Sweeney Is the Latest Celebrity to Wear Alo Yoga Activewear

 Posted on October 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  396 words  |  Martina Birk

This time of year, we gravitate toward our legging and bra dresser drawers more than ever. We want easy-to-wear outfits that don’t take much thought to put together (honestly, we can’t be bothered) while still keeping us warm and comfortable.

One brand our editors love, and Hollywood stars look to for revamping our activewear lineups is Alo Yoga, and actress Sydney Sweeney is among the recent growing list of celebs who are wearing the brand’s popular leggings and sports bras in and out of the gym. 

Sweeney recently posted a photo on Instagram after attending a Solidcore Pilates class sporting the best-selling 7/8 High-Waist Airbrush Leggings and Airlift Intrigue Bra in matching black colors. The post-class pic showed her wearing the set layered under a tan cardigan and paired with a black handbag and fuzzy slippers, aka the perfect cool-weather uniform.    

Alo Yoga 7/8  High-Waist Airbrush Leggings

In addition to Sydney Sweeney’s latest outfit, other stars such as Katie Holmes, and Jennifer Garner, and supermodels like Alessandra Ambrosio and even Kendall Jenner have made the brand’s leggings, sweats, and sports bras part of their everyday ensembles. Olivia Wilde also enjoys the Airbrush Leggings that Sweeney opted for in her latest workout ‘fit. Wilde’s been seen wearing them to and from gym sessions and has also been photographed in the Airlift Intrigue Bra as well.  

Alo Yoga  Airlift Intrigue Bra

The leggings are a best-seller for a reason. Over 1,400 shoppers have sung their praises, with five-star reviews raving about the style being their go-to for pilates, yoga, and casual everyday wear. One reviewer said they offer “nice compression” for a “flattering” shape. Another person called them “comfortable” since the “material breathes while [they] sweat.”

For a matching set like Sweeney’s, grab the Airlift Intrigue Bra that comes in a slew of colors ranging from neutrals to bright teal and periwinkle. The crisscross-style bra is “comfy yet supportive,” according to one shopper, and it provides light support for low-impact workouts like yoga, pilates, and, of course, ‘hot girl walks.’ The cups are also removable for your ideal shape. 

For Sweeney’s exact workout set, head over to Alo Yoga and keep scrolling for other shopper-loved legging and bra styles.
