Georges St-Pierre has been a UFC champion a total of two times. He held the welterweight title from 2006 to 2007 and then again from 2008 to 2013.

Georges St-Pierre is known for his well-rounded fighting style, which incorporates a mix of striking, wrestling, and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. His ability to seamlessly transition between different disciplines has made him a formidable opponent in the octagon.

Yes, Georges St-Pierre has lost on two occasions in his professional MMA career. He suffered a loss in his second professional fight and also faced defeat against Matt Serra in a shocking upset in 2007. However, he avenged the loss to Serra in a rematch later that same year.

4. Is Georges St-Pierre retired from MMA?

As of now, Georges St-Pierre is considered retired from active competition. He officially announced his retirement in February 2019, but there have been rumors of a potential comeback. However, as of now, he remains retired.
