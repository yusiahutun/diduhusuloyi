Urban Decay Naked Skin Powder Foundation

 Posted on March 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  233 words  |  Larita Shotwell

What they say: A feather-light texture and a matte finish that lasts for eight hours - big claims from Urban Decay. The brand promises that this new formula will blur fine lines for the appearance of fresh, perfected skin.

What we say: Ask any make-up artist and they will have an Urban Decay product in their kit. Whether it's the Naked eyeshadow palette or the super-strong make-up fixing spray, the brand has a cult following – and for good reason. The formulas are highly pigmented and long-lasting, without it looking as though you've caked on your make-up.

Usability: It's hard to go wrong with this foundation, unless you get the wrong shade. The texture is light and the coverage is buildable. Make sure your skin is fully hydrated and apply the powder with a brush rather than the sponge, so you can really buff it into the skin.

Results: Skin looks naturally radiant and the tone is evened out. You can layer the powder in the areas where you need slightly more coverage. 

Insider tip: Don't forget to add a touch of blusher to your cheeks, it will really help to lift your complexion and give you a healthy glow.

Urban Decay Naked Skin Ultra Definition Powder Foundation, £26, Urbandecay.co.uk
