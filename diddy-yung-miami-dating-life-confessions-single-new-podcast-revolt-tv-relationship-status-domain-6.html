Diddy tells Yung Miami he's 'single but dating', fans say she talking like he finna marry her

 Posted on September 14, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  654 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Rapper and entrepreneur P Diddy aka Sean Combs is rumored to be dating City Girls rapper and singer Yung Miami since the middle of the last year. While the Bad Boy Records boss has never addressed his dating headlines, he finally sat with the lady herself on her new podcast called ‘Caresha Please’.
 
Diddy was the very first guest on the show hosted by Yung Miami whose real name is Caresha Romeka Brownlee. The podcast was aired on Revolt TV which is owned by Diddy. The 52-year-old hip-hop veteran and Miami discussed their relationship on the show while also maneuvering through other topics. Diddy, whilst speaking about his dating life said that he was single but he was also dating which quickly left the Internet amused.

Halfway through the interview, Miami asks Diddy about his relationship status to which he responds, “I’m single, but I’m dating.” She followed up with her now-signature line, “So what we is”. To this Diddy adds, “We date. We’re dating. We go have dates, we’re friends,” concluding, “We go to exotic locations, we have great times. We go to strip clubs. Church.”
 
Yung Miami decided to address Diddy’s new remarks saying, “You single, but we go together. So how would you feel if I be telling people that I’m single cause we go together?” Sharing a hearty laugh together, the female emcee later made it clear it was no laughing matter to her.  Diddy says, “Hold on,” to which Miami adds, “It ain’t no hold on.” Diddy then asked, “We go together now?” To this Miami says “Real bad.” Diddy seems to agree with her saying, “Whatever you say” to which the City Girl answers, “I like that.”

‘She talking like he finna marry her’

The quirky confession by Diddy led to a laugh riot on Twitter as one of the users stated, “You single but I feel we go together’ looool Diddy just had to agree.” Another shared, “Yung Miami didn’t like diddy saying he was single but she was trying hard to keep that smile on her face lmfao.”  Whereas, one chimed in, “Caresha was SICKKKK when diddy said he was single.”
