The Raven Tree Collectibles - God Of War Ragnarok Wiki Guide

 Posted on July 25, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  694 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

God of War Ragnarok Raven Tree is a small region in the Niflheim Realm, which features a big tree filled with Odin’s Raven spirits in its chilly white setting.

To obtain the collectibles in GoW Ragnarok Raven Tree, you’ll have to progress with The Eyes of Odin Favour we’ve covered here. Below are listed all Raven Tree collectibles available:

  • 6 Legendary Chests
  • 1 Favor (The Eyes of Odin)

To reach The Raven Tree region, go to a Mystic Gateway, select the Niflheim Realm, and fast travel to Mystic Gateway in this region.

Now, let’s see how you can obtain all Raven Tree collectibles in God of War Ragnarok.

Legendary Chests 1-3: On The Left Side

From the Mystic Gateway in The Raven Tree region, follow the path ahead until you reach the big tree filled with the spirits of the ravens you’ve killed.

Open the first GoW Ragnarok Raven Tree Legendary Chest on the left to receive the Girdle of the Raven Tears, a Waist Armor. You must find and kill 6 Odin’s Ravens to unlock it.

Open the second Legendary Chest to receive the Bracers of Raven Tears, a Wrist Armor. You must find and kill 12 Odin’s Ravens to unlock it.

Open the third Legendary Chest to receive the Cuirass of Raven Tears, a Chest Armor. You must find and kill 18 Odin’s Ravens to unlock it.

Legendary Chests 4 & 5 & 6: On The Right Side

Open the fourth God of War Ragnarok Raven Tree Legendary Chest on the right side to receive the Breath of Thamur, a Heavy Runic Attack for your Leviathan Axe. You must find and kill 28 Odin’s Ravens to unlock it.

Open the fifth Legendary Chest to receive the Meteoric Slam, a Heavy Runic Attack for your Blades of Chaos. You must find and kill 38 Odin’s Ravens to unlock it.

Open the sixth Legendary Chest to receive The Finger of Ruin, a Heavy Runic Attack for your Draupnir Spear. You must find and kill all 48 Odin’s Ravens to unlock it.

The Eyes Of Odin (Favour): After Opening All Legendary Chests

After you open all six God of War Ragnarok Raven Tree Legendary Chests, a boss called The Raven Keeper spawns near you, then runs into the arena next to the Blacksmith Shop.

To easily defeat The Raven Keeper, it is recommended to have at least Gear Level 8. So, upgrade all your gear at the nearby Blacksmith Shop and grab a Ressurection Stone as well.

You can also change the difficulty in the settings and enable the midfight checkpoints in the accessibility menu.

Defeating The Raven Keeper will complete The Eyes of Odin Favour, unlocking it in the region overview.

The boss will also drop a Relic called Hilt of Forsbrandr, which is required for the Collector Trophy.

That’s every The Raven Tree collectible you can find and collect in God of War Ragnarok.
