The thing about Kenya Moore is that no matter how busy her schedule gets — filming Real Housewives of Atlanta, running her new Kenya Moore Hair Care line, plus a host of other side hustles — this former Miss USA crown holder keeps it all together by halting the go-go-go for mom-daughter dinnertime.

Her secret to making the holidays special for her daughter Brooklyn? Heart-shaped pizza from Home Run Inn!

“Spending time with Brooklyn to cook, play, and connect is everything to me,” Moore shares of her daughter. What’s her trick to balancing what Brooklyn desires and a healthful dinner? A freezer full of Home Run Inn Pizzas. “They hit every mark — all-natural, ready on the fly, and Brooklyn never says no her favorite pizza!”

For Kenya and Brooklyn, this mom/daughter balancing act seems to be working out just fine.

Home Run Inn is a proud, family-owned business—one with deep roots in the city of Chicago. In 1923, the original Home Run Inn location opened as a small tavern on Chicago’s South Side. Founded by Mary and Vincent Grittani, the tavern received its name one fateful day when a baseball from the neighborhood park smashed through one of the tavern’s windows, a home run for some young slugger on the sandlot. In 1947, after that baseball made history, Mary and her son-in-law, Nick Perrino, crafted the iconic Chicago pizza recipe known today.
