Charli XCX leaves little to the imagination as she rocks a revealing pink fishnet bodysuit for Gen V

 Posted on May 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  586 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Charli XCX was featured on the cover of Gen V's Fall Preview 2022, which was released on Friday.

The 29-year-old performer left little to the imagination during the photoshoot, as she rocked a very revealing fishnet outfit while striking a particularly sultry pose.

The hitmaker also sat down for an interview with the media outlet and discussed various aspects of her personality and professional life.

Front and center: Charli XCX was featured on the cover of Gen V 's Fall Preview 2022, which was released on Friday

Charli wore a pink fishnet bodysuit that conformed tightly to her curvy frame and featured a cut-out portion at her upper chest during the photoshoot.

The Boom Clap singer added a bit of extra glam to her outfit with a set of platform-heeled shoes that matched the tone of her clothing.

During her sit-down with the magazine, Charli spoke about what she had learned from generational differences.

'I think there are pros and cons to every generation. What's important is that we all learn from each other and also just live the best life we all can with the time we've got,' she said.

The hitmaker then stated that she was finally looking forward to changing her lifestyle, given the new developments in her personal life. 

Speaking her mind: During her sit-down with the magazine, Charli spoke about what she had learned from generational differences; she is seen in 2018

'Now that I'm happier as a person, and have found the love of my life, can appreciate that I've worked hard for what I have for many many years, I kinda just wanna slow down and take it all in,' she said.

Charli did point out, however, that she both enjoyed her role as a performer and was interested in going off the grid for a brief period of time.

She stated: 'Yeah, I'm addicted to work, but sometimes I just want to run away and spend all my money on stupid s*** and never be seen or heard from again!'
