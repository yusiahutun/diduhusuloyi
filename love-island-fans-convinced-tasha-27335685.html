However, it looks like the couple could be on the rocks as even though she wasn't chosen by the new boy Charlie, Tasha could have had her head turned by him.

Despite her naughty escapades, Tasha’s dad says he supports her no matter what.

Tarek Ghouri has been openly supporting his daughter on Facebook, sharing pictures and other assets, and gushing about his daughter’s activity on the programme in several posts.

Despite her controversial actions in Mallorca, Tarek doesn’t mind what’s going on in the villa as feels nothing but pride in his daughter.
