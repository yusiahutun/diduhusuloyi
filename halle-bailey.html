Biography Timeline

Halle Bailey is an American singer and actress best known as one half of the R&B duo Chloe x Halle and for her upcoming role as Ariel in the live-action adaptation of Disney’s “The Little Mermaid.” She was born on March 27, 2000, in Atlanta, Georgia. Here is a detailed timeline of her life and career.

  • March 27, 2000: Halle Bailey is born in Atlanta, Georgia.
  • 2012: Bailey and her sister Chloe start posting covers of popular songs on YouTube, gaining a following and catching the attention of Beyoncé.
  • 2015: The sisters sign to Beyoncé’s record label, Parkwood Entertainment, and release their debut EP, “Sugar Symphony.”
  • 2016: Chloe x Halle released their second EP, “The Two of Us,” and performed at the White House for President Obama.
  • 2018: The duo releases their debut album, “The Kids Are Alright,” which earns critical acclaim and a Grammy nomination.
  • 2019: Bailey is cast as Ariel in Disney’s live-action adaptation of “The Little Mermaid,” causing controversy due to the character’s traditionally white portrayal.
  • 2021: Bailey stars in the lead role of Jasmyn in the drama film “The Georgetown Project,” and Chloe x Halle release their second album, “Ungodly Hour,” which is also met with critical acclaim and a Grammy nomination.
  • 2022: Bailey is set to star as Tinkerbell in the live-action adaptation of “Peter Pan and Wendy.”

Halle Bailey’s talent and hard work have led to a successful career in music and acting, and she continues to break barriers and push for more representation in the entertainment industry. She and her sister Chloe have become known for their soulful harmonies and empowering lyrics, and Bailey’s upcoming roles in major Disney productions are sure to solidify her place in Hollywood.

Career

Halle Bailey and her sister started their YouTube channel, where they mutually posted a cover of songs they liked. On December 22, 2013, the sisters published a cover of Beyoncé’s “Pretty Hurts” – it became a turning point in their career.

The cover went viral and caught the attention of Beyoncé. Beyoncé took their video to her feed, and the video then was all over the internet. The duo debuted their talk show when they appeared on The Ellen Show in April 2012. Radio Disney crowned them as the winners of the fifth season of The Next Big Thing in December 2012. Consequently, They made a cameo appearance in the Disney series Austin & Ally, performing the song “Unstoppable” in September 2013.

Chloe x Halle made their professional debut with the EP Sugar Symphony, released under Parkwood on April 29, 2016. In the spring of 2017, Chloe x Halle released their mixtape, The Two of Us, which featured new music primarily written and produced by the duo.

In July 2019, Disney announced that Halle Bailey had been cast as Princess Ariel in the upcoming live-action remake of The Little Mermaid, which Rob Marshall directs. She will also record and perform the soundtrack for the film. On May 14, 2020, the duo released the single “Do It” and announced their second album, Ungodly Hour. The next day, “Forgive Me” was announced as the second single, released along with a video.

They released Ungodly Hour on June 12, 2020, to critical acclaim. The album debuted at number 16 on the Billboard 200 chart, with 24,000 units sold. “Do It” also became their first entry on the Billboard Hot 100, debuting at number 83 in June 2020. On February 24, 2021, a music video for the album’s title track, “Ungodly Hour”, was released.
