The top 10 most expensive belt brands in the world 2021

 Posted on May 8, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1464 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Have you ever wondered why some belts are pricey and some cheap? They are a fashion statement since they can add a sharp look to any outfit. Furthermore, belts are costly because the leather, design, and manufacturing costs depend on the brand. The most expensive belt brands use high-quality materials and decorate them with hard-to-find ornaments or metals.

A belt is a necessity for many people. Men have used it as an essential accessory. Nobody wants to compromise with its style and quality. Some of the most expensive belt brands use platinum, gold, and diamonds to make their products. Others use leather from animals like the crocodile, alligator, and even python.

Most expensive belt brands

The material with which a company uses to make its belts decides the price. That is one of the reasons you will find a belt going for over $1,000. Here are ten of the most expensive belt in the world.

1. Roland Iten - $400,000

In 2011, Roland Iten collaborated with Bugatti to make the most expensive belt buckle in the world. Dubbed Roland Iten Calibre R822 Predator, the white gold buckle featured red gold accents. It was also set with baguette-cut and round-cut diamonds that totalled 14.15 carats.

Its platform came from high-end titanium, and its price was an incredible $400,000. The company only made three pieces. Roland Iten does not make belts. However, it makes belt buckles considered to be expensive in the world.

For example, you can buy the R8 Mark II Driver buckle for CHF 32’300, which is an equivalent of around $41,000. However, you will be getting an accessory composed of solid white gold with titanium and has 30 pieces in different finishes. Only five buckles are available for sale.

2. Gucci - $256, 970

Gucci once commissioned the most expensive belt in the world, valued at $256,970. Dubbed Gucci diamond belt, the company used platinum and diamond materials and studded them into the fabric. The belt has 30 carats of diamonds that sit pretty on the signature double G symbol.

Gucci has continued to have an expensive collection of men’s belts. The Reversible Belt with Square G buckle costs $590. It has shiny gold-toned hardware and contains leather made using metal or chrome-free tanning process.

3. Stefano Ricci - $124,000

Stefano Ricci designs some of the most expensive designer belts in the world. All its men’s belts are handmade to demonstrate quality and detail. In the past, the company designed an 18-karat-gold and diamond-encrusted belt buckle worth $124,000.

Currently, its most expensive designer belt is the Handmade Crocodile Belt that costs $4,000. The company used genuine crocodile leather to make the strap and galvanised palladium to make the buckle. The result is an elegantly looking belt that can match any outfit or suit.

4. Hermes Entriviere - $5,260

Hermes Entriviere is one of the most expensive belts brands in the world. It has a robust collection of belts made from supple leather and infused with different colours on either side to give reversible wearing styles. Its buckles go through extensive metalwork and are all hand-polished. Take an example of the Constance belt buckle & Leather strap.

The belt costs $5,260. Its buckle comes in brushed palladium plated metal and the strap in matte crocodile leather. One reason it is pricey is the item belongs to the company's collection of customisable belts. It does not sell buckles separately. However, one can buy the straps individually and combine them with a clip of their choice to suit their requirements.

5. Billionaire Couture - $2,850

Billionaire Couture has been making extravagant belts for men for many years. One of its most expensive belts was the Billionaire Italian Couture Alligator belt. The men’s accessory came from an alligator skin and used the traditional Italian design to shape it. It also had a silver buckle, which pushed its price to $2,850.

As one of the famous belt brands, Billionaire Couture continues to make expensive men’s accessories. The company only targets rich men, hence the reason its products are pricey. Today, none of its belts goes for less than $400. Its priciest feature is the San Antonio Strass belt made from crocodile leather and an oval metal buckle. It costs $1,980.00.

6. Cartier - $2,690

Cartier is a brand that has made an indelible mark on the world of luxury culture and jewellery. The company crafts each piece with great details and uses high-quality materials and precious stones to design the final product. As a result, Cartier has an elegant collection of expensive belts globally. It also explains why the prices of its products are high.

For example, the Santos de Cartier belt cost $2,690. The company used black crocodile skin to make the strap. It has a golden-finish ardillon with the Cartier signature to demonstrate class.

7. Ralph Lauren - $1,850

Unlike other belt brands, Ralph Lauren designs belts that target men from different social classes. You can buy a designer belt for as low as $50. But on the high end, the company has a collection of some of the most expensive men’s belts.

For example, the Alligator Sterling-Buckle Belt costs $1,850. It is a versatile and elegant accessory made from alligator leather. On the mid-range is the Stirrup Turn-Back Suede Belt that costs $595. The company crafted it from supple calf suede and has a palladium-finish stirrup with a turn-back buckled closure.

8. Versace - $1,150

Versace belts add a touch of luxury to every man’s looks. The company crafts the accessory in Italy using the finest materials. It embellishes its leather belts with iconic buckles inspired by its rich heritage.

The Palazzo Croc Leather Belt is an example of a luxurious item in the men’s collection. It is a belt crafted from crocodile leather and embellished by a gold-tone Medusa buckle. You will have to part away with $1,150 to buy the accessory.

9. Salvatore Ferragamo - $995

Salvatore Ferragamo is one of the most esteemed brands in high-end fashion. Since the 1920s, it has specialised in making high-quality leather goods and accessories. The brand has an extensive line of men’s belts that go for thousands of dollars. Its collection of high-end belts includes the adjustable Gancini belt.

The Adjustable Gancini belt goes for $995.00. The company crafted the accessory from premium crocodile leather and gave it a sleek ruthenium finish. It also has a refined combination of tones and textures, which exude a sophisticated allure. You can pair the belt with pants or a suit for special occasions.

10. Louis Vuitton - $870

Louis Vuitton is a brand that makes some of the most expensive belts for men. One thing that distinguishes it from other brands is its belts are more fashionable and classic. Furthermore, the company specialises in making belts based on seasonal trends.

Even though it does not use leather to make most of its belts, they are expensive because they have designs that speak timeless style and luxury. For example, the LV Shape 40MM Reversible Taurillon Puppets Belt costs $870. It has bright accents on the strap and leather patches that depict playful puppet characters and embossed with the iconic Monogram pattern.
