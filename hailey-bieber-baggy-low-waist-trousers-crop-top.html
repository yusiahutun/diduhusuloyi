Hailey Bieber Bares Her Midriff in Baggy Low-Waist Trousers and a Crop Top

 Posted on April 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  305 words  |  Martina Birk

Hailey Bieber's effortlessly cool California-girl style just got cooler.

The model took a break from Halloween festivities this past weekend to step out for a lunch date with husband Justin Bieber on Sunday afternoon. For the outing, Hailey embraced the big-pants, little-shirt trend with a pair of baggy ivory white trousers that hung low on her hips and a delicate white eyelet crop top. She finished the outfit with black accents, including an oversized leather blazer, platform loafers, and dark oval shades from Balenciaga.

The "Peaches" singer showed off his own style while accompanying his wife, wearing an Eminem graphic T-shirt, olive green cargo pants, a bubblegum pink beanie, and chunky white sneakers.

For Halloween this year, the Rhode founder channeled Laetitia Casta’s iconic rose wedding dress, which Casta wore at Yves Saint Laurent's haute couture runway show in 1999. The Garden of Eden–esque look involved baby pink roses cascading across her neck, chest, and hips, as well as a frilly pink train enveloping the lower half of her body.

For Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story, Hailey got real about the pressure fame puts on her to always appear well-dressed.

"I get photographed so much that I feel like sometimes I put pressure on myself," she said, explaining that this feeling is also due to her own sense of perfectionism. "Even if I’m just throwing on jeans and a T-shirt, I want it to be a dope pair of jeans and a great T-shirt!"
